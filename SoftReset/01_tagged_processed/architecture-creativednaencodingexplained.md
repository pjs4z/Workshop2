## Creative DNA Encoding Explained

Generic prompts push AI toward the most familiar cultural clichés, stripping away nuance and emotional depth. To counter this, the proposed method treats chosen artistic influences as creative DNA: each source is examined for its core visual, emotional, and narrative qualities, then abstracted into a compact semantic and aesthetic profile that captures its essential spirit.

When content is requested, the system translates the prompt into the same abstract space, retrieves the closest DNA profiles, and guides generation with those traits while blocking unwanted motifs. Ongoing feedback refines the reference library, keeping it faithful to the intended style. This disciplined retrieval-and-conditioning cycle replaces surface imitation with principled reinterpretation, ensuring every output carries authentic atmosphere, psychological complexity, and cohesive artistic identity.
