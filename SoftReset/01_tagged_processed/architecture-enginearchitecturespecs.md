## Engine Architecture Specifications

The Reset Brain is conceived as a unified therapeutic platform that fuses semantic intelligence, generative storytelling and adaptive coaching to shepherd users through a rigorously measured recovery journey. Its engines share a 150-dimension representation that encodes knowledge, emotion, narrative structure and bio-psycho-environmental context, enabling the system to interpret a user’s state in real time and respond with content that is both clinically relevant and brand-consistent.

Five cooperative engines operationalise this model. The Knowledge Engine absorbs raw material and refines it into atomic “quantums” that anchor the shared semantic graph. Drawing on that graph, the Stories Engine crafts voice-consistent narratives whose emotional arcs are tuned to each stage of recovery. The Recovery Engine tracks biological, psychological and environmental markers, issues personalised directives, supplies live coaching and activates crisis protocols when risk signals emerge. The UI Engine transforms all outputs into cinematic, interactive experiences, while the Graphics Engine generates supportive imagery that mirrors the user’s emotional trajectory. Event-driven messaging, a common voice registry and layered caching keep the suite coherent, responsive and observably safe. The result is an immersive environment designed to heighten engagement, accelerate behavioural change and maintain seamless brand identity across every touchpoint.
