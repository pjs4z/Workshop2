## Graphics Analyzer System Instructions

The engine systematically curates emblematic visuals, dissects their composition, lighting, texture, symbolism, and emotional tenor, then distills these findings into a unified semantic profile that captures each work’s aesthetic DNA with precision. By converting this profile into an abstract vector representation and storing it in a dedicated retrieval layer, the system creates a durable, searchable memory of visual intent.

During creation or search, the engine retrieves the most relevant profiles and injects their distilled traits into downstream prompts, compelling generative models to replicate the original mood, palette, and thematic resonance rather than surface-level motifs. This closed loop secures authentic, repeatable alignment with the chosen artistic vision, scaling nuanced visual language across projects while preserving creative depth.
