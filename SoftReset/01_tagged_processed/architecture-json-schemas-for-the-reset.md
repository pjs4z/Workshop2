## JSON Schemas for The Reset Order Core Engines

The platform unites three engines beneath an eight-layer knowledge architecture. The Recovery Engine synthesises each individual’s psychological, neurobiological and environmental profile with live indicators of dopamine fitness, motivation and relapse risk, then issues uniquely traceable directives whose provenance links back to codified doctrine, creating a closed loop between knowledge and action. The Stories Engine turns the same recovery signals into letters, scripts or rally calls, locking in a predefined voice, narrative framework and emotional arc so every piece feels consistent yet remains tightly personalised through fresh metrics and milestone events. Feeding them both, the Knowledge Engine disassembles incoming documents into cross-linked quanta tagged to the semantic layers, recording status for governance and optional vector fingerprints for similarity search.

Doctrine thus shapes directives, directives and progress colour the stories, and all three streams enrich the shared knowledge base. The result is a recovery experience that is simultaneously data-grounded, emotionally resonant and self-improving, driving reliable behaviour change for users and compounding strategic insight for the organisation.
