## Knowledge Quantum Schema

The Reset Order blueprint defines every artifact the organization publishes as a self-contained “knowledge quantum,” a unit that marries story craft with strategic intent. Each quantum carries a unique identity, declares the layer of the business it energizes—from immutable brand DNA through product narratives to real-time telemetry—and frames its purpose through a concise abstract, target audience, genre, and guiding voice. Within that envelope it embeds a mythic arc, explicit stakes, a contrarian insight, and a mapped emotional journey, then breaks the tale into ordered content blocks whose micro-segments are tagged for rhetorical device, tension, release, and intended feeling.  

Around this narrative core the model layers universal context: formal emotional goals and coverage tallies, quality and distinctiveness scores, cross-references to related quanta, and system metadata for versioning and performance. Conditional attributes refine the picture for each business layer—strategic pieces log applicability and effectiveness, voice documents track tonal coherence, service stories bind to product stages—so that every quantum can be audited, remixed, or extended without losing its narrative spine.  

By fusing mythic structure, emotional telemetry, and operational metadata in a single specification, the blueprint turns storytelling into an accountable, modular system. Creators gain a shared language to craft persuasive journeys; strategists gain traceability across initiatives; the brand gains a consistent, living voice.
