## Knowledge Quantum V1 Schema Overview

Every knowledge unit is captured as a single, story-centric object that carries a unique identifier, declares its place within one of several strategic layers, and embeds a compact abstract, genre, audience definition, and voice selection. Around this core sit the elements that turn data into narrative: a mythic structure with stated stakes and contrarian insight, a sequenced plot outline, an emotional trajectory with measured intensity shifts, and granular content blocks that map each cognitive beat to the techniques and feelings it is meant to spark.

The framework deepens control by pairing each narrative with target emotional goals and a coverage report that counts how often those goals are actually hit. It records global style signals such as voice signature and distinctiveness scores, binds in layer-specific metadata for DNA, strategy, voice, content, user context, telemetry, aura, or service concerns, and links to related knowledge units across layers. Operational fields log processing versions, timestamps, quality scores, and semantic signatures so that every object can be audited, compared, and remixed without losing provenance.

By uniting structural storytelling, brand cohesion, emotional engineering, and machine-readable oversight in one construct, the model ensures every piece of knowledge advances a consistent narrative, meets defined experiential standards, and stays traceable from creation through deployment.
