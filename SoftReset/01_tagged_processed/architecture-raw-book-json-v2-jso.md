## Raw Book JSON v2 Schema Overview

The model establishes a single, linear stream of content blocks that renders any document—from simple narrative to highly technical text—into a standardized sequence. Each block declares its own structural identity and embeds concise semantic tags that clarify narrative role, knowledge function, and contextual nuance. Headings, paragraphs, lists, quotations, code samples, and tables coexist in this uniform array, all governed by the same minimal contract: state what you are, supply your content, and describe your intent.

A parallel metadata layer situates the entire document within a broader knowledge framework, specifying its strategic tier, genre, tonal stance, thematic signals, and any elements requiring special treatment. By collapsing form, meaning, and context into a compact, machine-legible package, the schema removes ambiguity, streamlines downstream processing, and unlocks consistent authoring, analysis, and reuse across platforms.
