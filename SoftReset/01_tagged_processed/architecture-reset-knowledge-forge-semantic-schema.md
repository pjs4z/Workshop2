## Reset Knowledge Forge – Semantic Schema Design Blu

Reset Knowledge Forge structures all brand intelligence into six versioned namespaces that progress from immutable foundations to live analytics. The dna_v1 layer anchors the system with the brand’s unchanging identity—mission, values, mythos, lexicon—each entry richly tagged so every downstream asset can trace its lineage back to canonical truth. Strategy_v1 transforms that DNA into voice-neutral messaging frameworks: outlines that define objectives, key messages, narrative arcs, and success metrics for any given campaign or context, ready to be styled later without rewriting the core ideas. Voices_v1 then captures the persona library—detailed linguistic, emotional, and stylistic blueprints for every character the brand may speak through—allowing any strategy to be expressed in a perfectly consistent tone.

Content_v1 emerges at the intersection of strategy and voice: each piece records its title, abstract, block-level text, emotional trajectory, narrative devices, quality scores, and a compound signature that concatenates all relevant semantic facets. These signatures, shared across layers, enable precise vector search and ensure every paragraph can be remixed or retrieved with full contextual awareness. User_ctx maintains individualized profiles—demographics, journey stage, preferences, transcripts, coach notes—so the system can tailor content and coaching in real time, injecting personal tokens and honoring each user’s motivational style. Telemetry completes the loop by logging every interaction, feedback signal, system event, and performance metric, feeding continuous learning that refines strategies, voices, and user context alike.

Unified tagging binds the layers together: identical facet vocabularies for tone, voice, journey stage, program method, and emotion allow cross-namespace queries that surface exactly the right snippet, outline, or user insight. In practice, a content request pulls a relevant strategy framework, overlays a selected persona voice, folds in user context, and generates a tagged piece that enters content_v1; ensuing telemetry measures its impact and updates user_ctx, while aggregate analytics suggest adjustments to voice patterns or strategic emphasis. One canonical truth informs many strategies, each strategy powers countless voice-styled outputs, and every user interaction guides the next iteration—producing a living, self-optimizing knowledge forge that scales founder intent with unwavering coherence.
