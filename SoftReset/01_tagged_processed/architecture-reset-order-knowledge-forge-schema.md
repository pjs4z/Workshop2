## Reset Order Knowledge Forge – Schema Design

Reset Order establishes a unified knowledge architecture that treats every piece of content—from first-principle mission statements to branded narratives and raw source text—as part of one coherent system. Foundational material describing purpose, pain, audience and hypothesis is captured in a concise question-and-answer framework that crystallizes core convictions and strategic assumptions. Higher-level brand and service stories are expressed through a narrative model that records mythic arc, emotional trajectory, stylistic voice and cross-references; this ensures each story advances clear stakes while remaining semantically linked to adjacent layers. Day-to-day text such as books, articles or reference notes is preserved in a streamlined block format enriched by lightweight semantic annotations so meaning can be surfaced without disrupting natural reading order.

Across all layers the schemas share a common grammar of tags and metadata, allowing material to be indexed, compared and recombined while still honouring its native form—from analytical foundation to emotive storytelling to linear prose. This design gives Reset Order the ability to track provenance, measure emotional impact, align brand voice, and route content to any medium with minimal friction, turning the knowledge base into a living engine for strategy, creativity and user engagement.
