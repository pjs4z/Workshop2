## Semantic Stack and Orchestration Schema

A tiered architecture channels fixed mission and values into tangible user transformation. A purpose layer secures the guiding narrative and staged journey, a scientific layer supplies evidence through archetypes, relapse models and recovery frameworks, and a creative layer locks voice, mythic structure and visual grammar so every artifact feels unmistakably aligned. An orchestration layer then chains modular services that turn intent into essays, videos or coaching prompts, relying on reusable blueprints, outlines, drafts, scene plans and storyboards to maintain coherence from concept to finished media.

Building on this core, a twelve-layer semantic stack stores all content in a unified vector space tagged with voice, emotional tone, recovery stage and the biological-psychological-environmental “three-frontier” model. Two calibrated voice archetypes blend to match each user’s capacity, while a recovery engine selects frontier-balanced micro-interventions, tracks momentum scores and triggers crisis protocols when risk escalates. A stories engine converts telemetry into personalized hero narratives, and a knowledge forge mines new data to refine guidance. Real-time event streams, strict governance and continuous learning close the loop, yielding a system that speaks with consistent resonance, delivers evidence-based micro-steps and evolves its strategy through measurable feedback.
