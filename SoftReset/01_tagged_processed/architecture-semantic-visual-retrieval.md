## Semantic Visual Retrieval

The system converts written narratives into fully formed video storylines by mapping each text’s emotional trajectory, symbolic motifs, and plot stages to a curated bank of lighting styles, color schemes, character archetypes, and scene frameworks. This semantic pairing yields a sequence of visuals and matching short-form scripts that arrive production-ready, eliminating most manual art-direction tasks. As a result, large volumes of brand-consistent videos can be generated quickly from raw text, giving creators scalable, schema-free control over tone, pacing, and thematic coherence.
