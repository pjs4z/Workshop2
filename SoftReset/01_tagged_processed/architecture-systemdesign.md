## Minimum Viable Product System Design

The minimum viable product concentrates the experience into five coordinated pillars that together express the program’s essence while remaining technically lean. A concise, emotive onboarding tale draws each participant into a personalised quest, establishing purpose from the first screen. Daily text exchanges with Coach Bear then maintain momentum through tailored prompts and challenges, while an always-open channel with the dating mentor offers specialised guidance on relationships and confidence. Live group huddles delivered through a familiar video-meeting service supply real-time brotherhood without bespoke infrastructure, and a simple progress indicator—day count, streak, and task acknowledgment—makes advancement visible and habit-forming.

This configuration delivers inspiration, accountability, expert counsel and communal support with minimal build, allowing rapid validation before adding richer media, further mentors or custom community tools. Users enter through story, stay for daily coaching, reach out for focused advice, connect face-to-face with peers and witness their own forward movement, gaining the core value the full platform intends to scale.
