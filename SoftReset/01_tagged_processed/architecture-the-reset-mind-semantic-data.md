## The Reset Mind Semantic Data Architecture

The system runs on four interlocking semantic layers, each charged with a distinct mandate that collectively safeguards the mission of liberating men from dopamine dysregulation. At the foundation sits the DNA layer, a real-time alignment engine that interrogates every proposed action with a single, non-negotiable question—how does this serve the mission? It houses the canon of mission statements, core values, problem framings, archetype profiles, solution hypotheses, and documented anti-patterns, enabling fast, binary judgments on strategic fit.

Above it, the Brain layer operates as the cognitive backend. This is the repository of hard science and validated methodology: dopamine mechanics, addiction psychology, recovery frameworks, and the evidence that stitches them together. Whenever another layer needs authoritative content or a factual ruling, Brain supplies it through structured queries, ensuring that every therapeutic claim or program element withstands scientific scrutiny before it reaches the outside world.

The Aura layer translates knowledge into experience. Drawing content from Brain, it chooses the voice, imagery, and emotional cadence that will cut through noise and land with the target archetype. Copy, image prompts, outlines, even chatbot flows are produced here, each passage stress-tested for brand consistency and motivational clarity before release.

Commanding the entire flow is the Services layer. Acting as master orchestrator, it defines output schemas, routes jobs, enforces quality gates, and moves finished assets through the delivery pipeline. By owning both workflow logic and final specification, it guarantees that every artifact—textual, visual, or programmatic—arrives complete, compliant, and on time.

A layer-centric project structure anchors this architecture in code. Dedicated directories for DNA, Brain, Aura, and Services keep mission validation, scientific reasoning, creative execution, and orchestration cleanly separated, while a shared utility module handles cross-cutting concerns such as authentication, storage, and monitoring. The result is a coherent, mission-first system whose every pathway leads back to sovereignty.
