## Updated Foundation Schema for DNA & Brain Layers

We have set a ten-year mission to overturn a pervasive, long-ignored form of addiction that silently cripples high achievers. The pain is visceral and urgent, and our own histories keep it non-negotiable. We hold a contrarian conviction that prevailing treatments miss the mark, and we calibrate our resolve on a scale that forces us to pursue disruption, not accommodation.  

To navigate this terrain we map a spectrum of archetypes, isolating for each the primary wound, the hidden self-judgement that magnifies it, the vice that offers short-lived relief, and the relationships that, though different in intention, tighten the spiral. Severity scores enable comparative triage and make progress measurable across cases.  

Guided by a single unifying hypothesis, we translate insight into a clear product pathway grounded in the psychology of trauma, conditioning and reward circuitry. Relapse is treated as the pull of twin forces—dopamine imbalance and diminishing agency—so our program targets both objective neurochemical fitness and the slope of personal control. By exposing the structural gaps that undermine rehabs, peer groups and self-help regimens, we position this model as the first comprehensive answer equal to the scale of the problem.
