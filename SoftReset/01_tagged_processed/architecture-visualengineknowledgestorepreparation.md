## Visual Engine Knowledge Store Preparation

The initiative establishes a unified narrative backbone for visual generation by distilling selected reference films into compact “DNA” profiles that formalize their emotional tone, visual language, symbolism, and thematic focus across five recovery stages. Each profile is semantically encoded and indexed by journey phase, ensuring that every creative request can be answered with precise, stage-appropriate context rather than surface imitation.

During production, user prompts are translated into the same semantic space, matched to the relevant DNA, and used to guide composition, palette, mood, metaphor, and story cadence. This closed retrieval-and-synthesis loop supplies clear guardrails, keeps output stylistically coherent from despair to resolution, and enables rapid iteration without drifting into cliché or unwanted genres. The result is an engine that repeatedly delivers images and narratives carrying authentic emotional gravity and a traceable lineage back to the curated cinematic sources.
