## Cinematic Dark-First Design System Overview

The system defines a cinematic, dark-first visual language that casts each interface as a chapter in the user’s heroic recovery narrative. Grounded in trauma-informed practice and behavioral science, it balances commanding presence with nurturing warmth, unveils information in manageable beats, and rewards progress without shaming setbacks, all to safeguard autonomy and emotional safety.

Its framework aligns color, type, motion, and component patterns to the arc of descent, trial, and redemption. A purpose-driven palette moves from deep introspection through forge-bright challenge to golden triumph; complementary typographic voices alternate between sovereign authority and coach-like encouragement. Abstract tokens regulate spacing, rhythm, and transition tempo, while micro-interactions, iconography, and page transitions echo the journey’s emotional cadence. Accessibility rules for contrast, focus, and reduced motion anchor inclusivity at every layer.

By unifying these elements, the design system turns routine tasks into immersive storytelling that deepens focus, affirms agency, and continually signals possibility, guiding users from crisis toward sustained transformation.
