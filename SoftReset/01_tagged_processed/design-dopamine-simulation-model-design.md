## Dopamine Simulation Model Design

A minute-resolution simulation tracks every logged substance or behaviour across a day, classifying each by both its broad type and the physiological route through which it influences dopamine—whether provoking release, blocking clearance, boosting synthesis, acting through indirect pathways, evoking anticipatory surges, or fostering natural replenishment. These mechanistic signatures are convolved over time to build a continuous dopamine curve that rises with stimulation, falls as stores are spent, and self-corrects toward baseline through a homeostatic term. Parallel accounting of biochemical cofactors such as tyrosine and vitamin B6 gates the recovery rate, ensuring the model’s synthetic capacity mirrors real metabolic limits.

From the completed twenty-four-hour trace the engine extracts a Dopamine Health Score on a five-point scale and renders an intuitive trajectory visual. It then translates the curve’s inflection points into personalised guidance, highlighting overstimulation, nutrient gaps, sleep debt, or restorative successes and recommending concrete adjustments to sustain balanced neurochemistry. The overall goal is to give individuals a precise, actionable portrait of how daily choices shape dopamine resilience and therefore motivation, pleasure and self-control.
