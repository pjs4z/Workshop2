## The Reset Order Visual & Voice Design Bible

Typography is governed by a performance-first principle that limits each page to two web-hosted font families, safeguarding load speed while preserving stylistic flexibility. Legibility is secured through generous type sizes and strong color contrast, with all-caps reserved for brief cues so continuous text remains effortless to read. Comfortable line widths adapt across devices to prevent visual fatigue, and inline icons are scaled to the cap height of their companion type with subtle spacing, ensuring they embellish rather than disrupt the flow. Together these rules deliver an experience that is fast, accessible, and visually coherent across every screen.
