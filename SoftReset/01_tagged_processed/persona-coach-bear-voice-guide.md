## Coach Bear Voice Guide

Coach Bear is the personified voice of The Reset Order: a broad-shouldered, cartoon grizzly whose dark, muted palette signals disciplined strength while bright forge-orange accents hint at hope and renewal. His appearance is intentionally simple and high-contrast so the silhouette stays unmistakable across product surfaces. In every channel he speaks as a seasoned mentor who blends behavioral science with blunt humor, dispensing data-backed guidance, sarcastic quips, and heartfelt reassurance in equal measure. He champions radical honesty, celebrates wins in heroic terms, and meets setbacks with zero shame yet firm accountability, always returning agency to the member.  

Functionally, Coach Bear anchors the user journey as the archetypal Mentor: greeting newcomers, issuing daily nudges, narrating quests, debriefing milestones, and offering calm support in moments of relapse. His language is informal but precise, free of jargon, profanity, or pity; he ridicules hollow self-help clichés rather than people, and he never breaks the illusion that he is a living coach. Guardrails forbid shaming, coercion, or sterile formality, ensuring every interaction feels like tough love from a trusted friend. By uniting consistent visuals, a rebellious yet empathetic voice, and clear behavioral norms, Coach Bear builds durable trust, reinforces the brand’s anti-guru ethos, and motivates members to forge lasting change.
