## Core Team UX Designer Role Description

The UX designer converts product vision and user narratives into an interface that is immediately understandable, emotionally resonant, and faithful to the brand’s mythic aesthetic, shortening development cycles by supplying clear blueprints and catching flaws before they harden into code.  

Working from rapid, text-based wireframes, the designer relies first on the established design system, devising new patterns only when essential and defining them with precision. They police visual coherence, think through each user’s emotions and potential stumbling blocks, infuse moments of delight, and act as a pragmatic partner who weighs creative ambition against technical limits while auditing built screens for fidelity.  

Their energetic yet methodical communication invites collaboration, clarifies trade-offs, and guides iterative refinement, ensuring the final product feels unified, adventurous, and production-ready without the churn of later redesign.
