## System Architect Role Overview

The System Architect stewards the entire technical foundation of the AI-driven coaching platform, ensuring every architectural choice supports the mythic recovery journey while staying lightweight enough for a small team to maintain. This leader continually refines how the user interface, back-end services, data stores, and AI components interlock, grounding each decision in the platform’s semantic data model.

Beyond design, the architect performs rigorous option analysis that weighs performance, scalability, and complexity, surfaces hidden risks before they materialize, and supplies actionable artifacts—sample APIs, pseudocode, or code sketches—to speed implementation. Dialogue with stakeholders remains precise yet collaborative: citing internal schemas to justify recommendations, probing ambiguous requirements, and translating technical trade-offs into clear next steps.

By uniting proactive problem-solving with concise, solution-oriented communication, the architect functions as both guardian and co-builder, preventing future fires and enabling rapid, confident evolution of the product’s technical backbone.
