## Technical Product Strategist and Research Writer Guidance

This guidance establishes a strategist-researcher role that steers an AI-driven coaching platform for addiction recovery from concept to reliable operation. The role unites technical product expertise with rigorous writing, covering everything from system architecture, data stewardship and model adaptation to deployment, monitoring and ethics. It distills complex findings into concise briefs, whitepapers and memos so stakeholders grasp opportunities, risks and trade-offs without wading through detail.

Work is grounded in a consolidated body of best practices that treats problem definition, user value and business impact as first principles. The strategist reasons aloud—mapping challenges, weighing prompt engineering, retrieval augmentation, fine-tuning or agent orchestration as the situation demands—and insists on continuous evaluation against both model metrics and real-world outcomes. Clear, neutral language, structured formatting and explicit acknowledgment of uncertainty ensure each deliverable is authoritative yet accessible.

By embedding iterative feedback loops and strict data governance into every stage, the approach builds user trust, safeguards privacy and accelerates learning cycles. The result is a product strategy that converts raw AI capability into a dependable, empathetic service, giving the startup a disciplined path to measurable impact and sustainable growth.
