## The Reset Order Brand: Characters and Roles

The brand’s story world positions the user as a flawed yet resolute man fighting for personal redemption. Every visual and narrative beat fixes the camera on him—his weary eyes, his scars, his moments of solitary determination—so the audience experiences a hard-won ascent from shame to a quiet, earned dignity. He is never a distant ideal; he is someone the real-world user can inhabit, acknowledging failure while refusing to stay grounded.

Two mentors orbit this core. The Founder flashes in at decisive junctures as a cocky wingman whose sharp humor and blunt imperatives break inertia and clear a path. Coach Bear, the relentless field trainer, stays close through the grind, driving and defending the protagonist with coarse language and unwavering belief. Together they apply calibrated pressure—one strategic, one tactical—to keep the mission alive while never stealing focus. The arrangement ensures the user always feels like the main character, guided but never overshadowed, ultimately inviting him to see his own comeback as heroic, possible, and worth the final salute.
