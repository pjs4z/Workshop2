## Enhanced Onboarding Flow and Comprehensive User Profile

The program treats addiction as a reversible pattern of learned behaviors rather than a fixed disease. Each harmful repetition strengthens neural pathways that erode self-control, while consistent daily improvements can rebuild that agency. Because sustained change hinges on motivation, the system is designed to reinforce the user’s willpower through emotionally resonant, habit-based interventions that compound over time.

Onboarding casts the newcomer as the hero of an unfolding saga. Through an open-ended video narrative the user describes origins, struggles, aspirations, turning points, and sources of inspiration. An AI engine transcribes and semantically analyses this story to distil biography, psychological traits, dopamine-related symptoms, environmental triggers, and preferred coaching styles, then situates the individual on a Hero’s Journey arc. These insights, combined with supplemental factual prompts, form a holistic profile that tracks dopamine fitness, motivation, relapse risk, and other dynamic indicators.

The profile becomes a living source of truth that powers all subsequent coaching. By linking narrative identity to real-time state, it allows the platform to deliver stage-appropriate content, assign personalized directives, and trigger timely interventions whenever risk rises or milestones are reached. The result is an adaptive, story-driven recovery experience that feels intimately tailored while remaining grounded in measurable neurobehavioral progress.
