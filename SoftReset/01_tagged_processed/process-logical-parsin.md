## Logical Parsing: Domain-Neutral Symbolic Proposition Extraction

Pure logical parsing converts any spoken or written statement into a domain-neutral web of symbolic propositions, retaining only content that the speaker has explicitly expressed. It isolates atomic claims, removes linguistic filler, and links those claims with standard logical connectives to reveal the direct premises, dependencies, and conclusions embedded in the original discourse—nothing more, nothing less.

This disciplined reduction yields a transparent blueprint that can be compared, audited, or extended without the distortion of inferred motives or contextual guesswork. By fixing the argument’s structure in a rigorously explicit form, the method supplies a stable substrate for downstream evaluation—whether in debates, contracts, strategic decisions, or meeting records—while clearly marking the point beyond which subjective interpretation must take over.
