## Selective Enrollment and Identity-Curated Cohorts

Reset welcomes accomplished men—leaders, professionals, founders—who arrive with baseline stability, a growth mindset, and an unambiguous commitment to change. Selectivity is never posturing; it creates rooms of true peers where shared experience dissolves judgment and establishes instant respect. Each small, deliberately matched cohort assumes the mantle of an Order, a confidential brotherhood founded on trust and unified goals. By framing recovery as a privilege and mark of character, the program supplies both psychological safety and an earned sense of prestige.

Identity-curated grouping—mid-career fathers here, early-stage entrepreneurs there—sharpens recognition and accelerates cohesion. Within days, the Order’s fraternity takes hold: members challenge, encourage, and model progress for one another, elevating collective momentum rather than allowing anyone to drift.
