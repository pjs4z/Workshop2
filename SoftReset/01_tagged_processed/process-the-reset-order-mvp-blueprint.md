## The Reset Order MVP Blueprint: Website User Journey

The platform embodies The Reset Order’s scientific framework as a modular, tool-first program that guides each member through four sequential phases—Onboarding, Stabilization, Recovery, and Performance—while maintaining a direct, supportive, action-oriented voice. From the first visit, the system captures baseline data, delivers an immediate personalized roadmap, and initiates a structured orientation that blends automated guidance with optional professional consultation, establishing trust and a clear sense of progress.

As members advance, daily self-reporting, routine planning, round-the-clock conversational coaching, targeted micro-interventions, and peer interaction create the scaffolding needed to weather early challenges and forge new habits. Once initial turbulence subsides, the experience shifts toward habit consolidation, goal refinement, skill-building lessons, relapse planning, and deeper human support, all tracked and adjusted by the same adaptive engine. In the final stage, members convert hard-won stability into high performance through stretch-goal design, periodic AI check-ins, leadership opportunities within the community, specialized optimization modules, and a formal graduation that locks in long-term maintenance.

By threading personalized data, evidence-based practices, constant availability of support, and a cohesive community ethos through every phase, the system moves users from chaos to sustained mastery while remaining flexible enough to evolve feature by feature. The result is a scalable digital experience that delivers clinical rigor, human empathy, and tangible empowerment without excess complexity.
