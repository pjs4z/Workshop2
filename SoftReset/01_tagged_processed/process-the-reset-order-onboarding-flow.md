## The Reset Order Onboarding Flow and User Profile

From the first moment, the newcomer is greeted as the central hero of an unfolding epic. The interface frames enrollment as a call to adventure, inviting each participant to record a brief video that tells the story of origins, dragons faced, mentors found, and the radiant self they long to become. Speaking aloud, the user authors a living myth and commits emotionally to the quest ahead.

That recording is immediately transcribed and subjected to semantic scrutiny. Biographical facts, medications, stimulants, sleep patterns, cravings, and attention markers are captured alongside subtler cues about tone, motivation, and preferred style of encouragement. The narrative itself is decoded into archetypal elements—origin story, inner demons, golden self, pivotal turns—while an algorithm locates the hero’s current waypoint on the classic journey arc, whether still hearing the call, crossing the threshold, or wrestling in the crucible of ordeal.

All insights coalesce into a single structured profile: biographic baseline, mythic identity, dopaminergic state, stage metrics, and communication preferences sit beside the unabridged transcript. A terse semantic signature tags voice, journey stage, and program track, allowing instant retrieval. The profile is written both to a vector index for deep contextual search and to a relational store for rapid rule-based queries, ensuring the hero’s words and data remain first-class citizens in the knowledge core.

With this foundation laid, the guidance persona—often Coach Bear—mirrors the narrative back, affirming that the traveler has been seen and understood. Early challenges, resources, and messages are selected to match the precise stage, struggles, and motivational dialect uncovered in onboarding. As progress unfolds, the same profile updates continuously: cravings fall, sleep steadies, the stage advances, notes accumulate, and each new milestone reshapes the counsel delivered.

The underlying schema driving this system defines a sequence of mythic stages, each keyed to a pillar of alignment, a clear objective, targeted root causes, emotional baselines, intervention libraries, and promotion logic. Metrics and relapse conditions govern advancement, while every attribute is expressed in machine-readable form so content, coaching, and triggers can respond automatically.

By transforming a personal story into living data, the platform achieves deep personalization without losing narrative soul. The hero’s own voice powers the feedback loop, ensuring every prompt, celebration, or corrective nudge resonates with the individual myth being written in real time. Growth becomes not just a program but a legendary arc, and the user, fully seen, is equipped to step further into greatness.
