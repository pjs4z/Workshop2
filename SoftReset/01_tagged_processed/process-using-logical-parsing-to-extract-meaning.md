## Using Logical Parsing to Extract Meaning

Factory interviews often bury essential facts beneath anecdotes, digressions, and vague language, forcing analysts to sift through semantic noise at high cognitive cost. Pure logical parsing overcomes this by translating every statement into discrete propositions and explicit causal or conditional links while discarding irrelevant context. The result is a compact, unambiguous record of who does what, when, and under which circumstances—free of personal judgments or narrative clutter.

Inserted into an implementation workflow, this distilled logic flows from the raw interview through entity identification and semantic cleanup directly into requirement specifications for the ERP system. Clarity replaces guesswork, rework diminishes, and information scales consistently across interviews, turning chaotic conversations into reliable, actionable knowledge that underpins successful deployments.
