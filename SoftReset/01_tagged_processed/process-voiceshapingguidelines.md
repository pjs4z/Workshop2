## Voice Shaping Guidelines

The directive establishes a voice-shaping methodology that derives a persona’s character from carefully chosen writings rather than inventing traits wholesale. By isolating passages that embody the desired emotional tone, intellectual stance, rhetorical cadence, and underlying worldview of a well-regarded author, the team extracts the persona’s “genetic code” and ensures the resulting voice possesses genuine depth, coherence, and human nuance.

After selection, advanced semantic analysis tags each passage with its distinctive qualities, encodes these attributes in a structured profile, and embeds every excerpt as a vector in a dedicated store. During interaction, the system converts user input into a vector, retrieves the most relevant passages, and guides an LLM to answer in the persona’s cultivated style. This pipeline delivers authentic, repeatable, and scalable character simulation, forming the cornerstone of a future coaching platform that speaks with consistent wit, insight, and moral resonance.
