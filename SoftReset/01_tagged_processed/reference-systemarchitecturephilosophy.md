## System Architecture Philosophy

Grand mission statements dilute originality; beginning with them has repeatedly pushed our work toward clichés. We now initiate every project by latching onto a single, vivid exemplar and extracting the exact traits that make it resonate. Those traits are codified in fine grain, then backed by a targeted sweep of research that yields a corpus rich enough to teach both humans and machines how to embody the chosen model. Only after that immersion do we design the system, letting real nuance guide architecture, prompts, and user experience.

This reference-driven sequence—exemplar, attributes, codification, corpus, design—replaces abstraction with specificity, guards AI outputs from generic drift, and aligns the entire team around a tangible source of truth. By hard-wiring this practice into our operating fabric, we aim to ship products whose voices feel lived-in, whose logic is grounded in proven insight, and whose impact outstrips anything born from broad ambition alone.
