## The Reset Order Copywriting Canon

The canon commands writing that detonates on contact: every sentence must strike, mirror, or blaze, fusing sly insider humor, surgical intellect, naked honesty, fleeting ache, and hard-won grit into a single emotional current that never slows. Momentum rules; scenes cut fast, surprises hit twice a page, and brief technical riffs expose the myths of addiction science or self-help orthodoxy so forcefully the reader can quote them in battle. Signature metaphors and a cultivated private vocabulary build a house style that feels like code shared among veterans, while meta-asides and direct challenges pull the audience into co-conspiracy.  

The voice speaks tribe to tribe—never lecturing, always inviting—framing struggle as trial, wins as mythic steps, and setbacks as fuel for return. Condescension, platitudes, and slack prose are forbidden; drafts must survive tests for punch, screenshot-worthy lines, and mind-changing facts. The result is not content but a frontline dispatch: raw, hilarious, and unmistakable, handed from one brother in the fire to the next man climbing out, ending with an open dare to reclaim the story together.
