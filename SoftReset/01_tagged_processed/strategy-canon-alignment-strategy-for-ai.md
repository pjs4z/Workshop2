## Canon Alignment Strategy for AI-Assisted Knowledge

The strategy establishes a single hierarchical canon that anchors all knowledge and decision-making. An immutable root states the mission—helping men with ADHD unlearn addictive behaviours through an AI-native coaching platform and peer community—and codifies convictions such as the learnability of addiction, the compounding power of small daily gains, and the indispensability of human connection. Every subsequent layer, from guiding principles to feature definitions and creative personas, must trace explicitly to this root, ensuring clarity and eliminating conceptual drift.

Knowledge blocks are semantically tagged and bidirectionally mapped so a change at any level propagates transparently upward for validation and downward for execution. Creative voices operate within documented reference DNA, remaining verifiable against their sources while never straying beyond canonical bounds. Regular alignment reviews interrogate new material against the hierarchy, correcting contradictions or, when justified, elevating fresh insights into higher-order truth.

By fusing rigorous knowledge governance with controlled creative latitude, the framework turns AI contributors into dependable teammates who generate code and content rapidly yet with one coherent voice. Developers gain an unambiguous compass that links every technical or narrative choice back to first principles. The result is a platform built swiftly and cohesively, where incremental work compounds like the daily wins it promotes and users encounter an experience that is consistently empathetic, empowering and scientifically grounded.
