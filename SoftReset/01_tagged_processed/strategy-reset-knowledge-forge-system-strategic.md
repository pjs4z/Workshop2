## Reset Knowledge Forge System – Strategic Specification

The Knowledge Forge distills the founder’s narrative essence into a living intelligence that grows sharper with every contribution. Each sentence the founder blesses becomes reusable DNA, encoding voice, tone, and mythic motifs so that any surface—app dialogue, blog post, social quip—arrives fully cinematic and unmistakably on-myth. The system learns in real time, turning isolated creative sparks into an ever-evolving canon that powers the Reset experience wherever it appears.

At its heart the founder remains the chief storyteller. Tagging a line or paragraph is swift and tactile: highlight, label, move on. AI quietly proposes tags, but never overrules; a single click elevates any passage to canonical status, the interface igniting with forge-like flare as the content enters the archive. Dark palettes, dramatic type, and subtle motion cues make every interaction feel like crafting legend by hand.

Behind the scenes a dual-mode dashboard pairs expert manual tagging with AI assist. Inline labels capture tone, emotional intensity, persona, motif, humor style, or any custom dimension the founder deems vital. One-click canonization forges a compact semantic signature—an elegant strand of code that travels with the piece—then persists both JSON structure and vector embedding in the knowledge store. A visual diff view exposes stylistic drift between versions, while a live alignment dial guards against tonal slippage the moment it appears.

The ingestion pipeline begins the instant new text arrives: embeddings are computed, provisional tags suggested, structural outlines inferred. As the founder refines those tags, a real-time schema preview takes shape, confirming exactly how the asset will live in the database. Approval triggers automatic storage, namespace routing, and a feedback loop that makes the next round of suggestions even smarter.

Flexible JSON schemas accommodate essays, dialogue, outlines, character profiles, and evolving rhetorical devices. Each asset carries a draft-final-legendary status, rich metadata, and lineage tracking. Schemas double as generation blueprints; when fresh content is requested, the Forge retrieves the most resonant outlines and voice signatures, hands them to the language model, and insists on structural and tonal fidelity before delivery. Anything that strays is corrected or escalated for human review.

Exposed through secure APIs, the Forge functions as an independent microservice, ready to feed the Reset app, marketing sites, or future platforms. Containerized deployment, CLI utilities, and event webhooks keep operations smooth, scalable, and observable. A unified dashboard reports coverage, alignment scores, drift trends, and vector index health, while an immutable audit trail records every act of canonization.

In essence, the Knowledge Forge lets a solo founder wield mythic consistency at industrial scale. AI shoulders the heavy lift, yet the human storyteller’s hand remains visible in every spark, ensuring the brand’s heroic voice rings true across all touchpoints.
