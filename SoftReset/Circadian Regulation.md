# Circadian Regulation

**Definition:** The maintenance of twenty-four-hour physiological cycles, specifically through sleep hygiene and rest practices. It is the biological foundation required to replenish receptor availability for proper dopaminergic functioning and executive control.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The "Homeostatic Restoration Loop."
- **Includes:** [[Yoga Nidra]] (Non-Sleep Deep Rest), Sleep Hygiene, Diet.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Behavioral choices, environmental light/dark cycles.
- **Downstream (Increases):** [[Executive Modulation]] capacity, [[Dopamine]] receptor sensitivity.
- **Failure State:** Sleep deprivation leads to reduced receptor availability ("traffic jam"), causing mood instability and loss of impulse control.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability (Physical):** The absolute prerequisite for behavioral change. You cannot "think" your way out of a dopamine loop if your biology is depleted.
- **Leverage Point:** Prioritizing sleep and stillness practices ([[Yoga Nidra]]) restores the brain's ability to say "no."
