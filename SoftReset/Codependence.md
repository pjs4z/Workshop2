# Codependence

**Definition:** A psychological construct describing individuals in relationships with addicts or troubled persons, characterized by an obsession with controlling the other person's behavior and a reliance on the relationship for self-worth.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Often part of the **Cycle of Dependence**, where the partner's behavior enables the [[Addiction]] to continue while degrading their own [[Value System]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Low [[Self-Efficacy]]; need for external validation.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Maintains the addictive system; prevents independent [[Natural Recovery]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** Identity derived from the role of "caretaker" or "savior."
- **Leverage Points:** Establishing boundaries and focusing on personal [[Self-Efficacy]].
