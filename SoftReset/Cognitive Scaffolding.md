# Cognitive Scaffolding

**Definition:** The use of external structures, digital tools (like Generative AI), or systems to offload executive function demands. It serves as a "prosthetic" for working memory, sequencing, and initiation, particularly for neurodivergent (ADHD) profiles.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Scaffolding shifts the locus of control from internal biology to external systems.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Executive Function Deficit]] (poor working memory, impulsivity).
- **Downstream (Drives):** Sustainable [[Deep Work]] by reducing "Attention Switching Costs."
- **Function:** Break macro-projects into micro-steps (Decomposition) to prevent paralysis.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Compensates for biological deficits in the Prefrontal Cortex.
- **Opportunity:** Generative AI acts as a real-time "co-pilot," maintaining the semantic frame when the user's attention wavers.
- **Leverage Point:** Use AI not just for content generation, but for *process* generation (breaking a large "frog" into actionable steps).
