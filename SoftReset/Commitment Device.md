# Commitment Device

**Definition:** A choice made in the present that restricts options in the future to lock in good behavior and make bad habits impossible or impractical.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Mechanism:** Inverts the [[Law of Least Effort]] for bad habits by increasing friction to infinity (making it impossible).
- **Function:** Counteracts [[Time Inconsistency]] by binding the future self to the rational decisions of the present self.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Need for self-control.
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Environment Design]], [[Social Norms]] (via accountability)

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Opportunity:** Removes the opportunity to fail.
- **Leverage Point:** Automating habits (e.g., automatic savings transfers) is the ultimate commitment device.
