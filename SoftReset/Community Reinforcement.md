# Community Reinforcement

**Definition:** A therapeutic strategy that modifies an individual's social environment, including family, work, and peers, to reward non-addictive behavior and remove support for substance use.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Acts as an exogenous driver for the **Virtuous Cycle of Natural Recovery**.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Social Engineering and policy.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Increases the strength of the [[Value System]] and supports [[Self-Efficacy]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Opportunity:** Social Opportunity (restructuring the social network).
- **Leverage Points:** Ensuring intimacy and approval are contingent on non-addictive behavior (e.g., Reciprocity Marriage Counseling).
