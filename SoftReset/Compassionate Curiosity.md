# Compassionate Curiosity

**Definition:** A non-judgmental stance of self-inquiry used to observe one's own addictive impulses, emotional pain, and behaviors. It replaces toxic shame (which fuels addiction) with an objective, kindly interest in the self, facilitating healing.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Compassionate Curiosity activates the "Impartial Spectator" in the mind.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Conscious intention and mindfulness practices.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Improved [[Self-Regulation]] and the ability to unlock [[Brain Lock]].
- **Mechanism:** It reduces the stress/cortisol response associated with self-blame, allowing the [[Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)]] to come back online.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** Moves the individual from reflective self-loathing (which leads to relapse) to reflective understanding.
- **Leverage:** Essential for the "Re-label" and "Re-attribute" phases of [[The Four Steps]].
