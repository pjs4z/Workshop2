# Control Circuit

**Definition:** A mesocortical pathway utilizing the frontal lobes to manage planning, abstract reasoning, and executive function. It allows an individual to manipulate abstract concepts and secure objectives identified by the [[Desire Circuit]].

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The Control Circuit converts the raw urge of the [[Desire Circuit]] into actionable plans. It enables [[Mental Time Travel]] and [[Convergent Thinking]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Dopamine]] (specifically in the prefrontal cortex).
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Mental Time Travel]], [[Convergent Thinking]], Executive Function, Delayed Gratification.
- **Relationship:** It works in tandem with the [[Desire Circuit]] but can be inhibited by extreme emotional states or [[Reactance]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Provides the cognitive hardware necessary to override immediate impulses.
- **Leverage Points:** Can be strengthened through tools like the Eisenhower Matrix (for prioritization) or [[The Observing Self]] (for emotional regulation).
