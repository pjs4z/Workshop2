# Convergent Thinking

**Definition:** A cognitive process governed by [[Control Circuit]] dopamine that focuses on filtering ideas, applying logic, and deriving a single effective solution from available options.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Control Circuit]], Logic, Constraints.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Execution, Solution implementation.
- **Relationship:** The necessary closer to [[Divergent Thinking]]. Without it, creativity is just chaos.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Requires high executive function and focus.
- **Leverage Points:** Tools like the Eisenhower Matrix help force Convergent Thinking by imposing structure on abstract options.
