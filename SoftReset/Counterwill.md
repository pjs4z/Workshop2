# Counterwill

**Definition:** An automatic resistance to external pressure, coercion, or authority, arising from an underdeveloped sense of self. In [[Attention Deficit Disorder]], it protects the fragile autonomy of the individual but prevents the internalization of necessary structure.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Implicit Memory]] of helplessness and intrusive parenting/schooling.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Procrastination, conflict with authority, and inability to follow even one's own plans.
- **Feedback Loop:** **The Counterwill Isolation Cycle**—Pressure triggers counterwill -> counterwill blocks guidance -> lack of guidance leads to failure -> failure invites more pressure.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** Acts as a massive block to "should-based" motivation.
- **Leverage Point:** **The Autonomy Clause**—Removing the "duty" aspect of tasks. Framing actions as free choices to support the self, neutralizing the resistance.
