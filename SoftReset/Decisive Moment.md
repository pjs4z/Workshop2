# Decisive Moment

**Definition:** A small choice that sets the trajectory for a longer block of time (e.g., putting on gym shoes). These moments constrain the options available to the future self.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Mechanism:** Acts as a "Gateway Habit." You must standardize the beginning of the behavior before you can optimize the rest.
- **Rule:** The **Two-Minute Rule** states a new habit should take less than two minutes to do, ensuring it complies with the [[Law of Least Effort]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Atomic Habit]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** Deep work, [[Automaticity]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Makes the habit so easy you can't say no.
- **Leverage Point:** Ritualize the entry point. Once the first 2 minutes are done, the rest of the routine often follows automatically due to momentum.
