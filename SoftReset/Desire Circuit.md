# Desire Circuit

**Definition:** A mesolimbic pathway starting in the ventral tegmental area that generates the visceral feeling of wanting, enthusiasm, and anticipation for things not yet possessed. It prioritizes immediate gratification and novelty.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The Desire Circuit is the engine of the **Addiction Escalation Loop**. It floods the brain with [[Dopamine]] in response to cues.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Dopamine]], [[Reward Prediction Error]], [[Reactance]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Impulsive behavior, Cravings, [[Downregulation]].
- **Balancing Loop:** The **Satisfaction Homeostasis Loop** relies on [[Here and Now (H&N) Neurotransmitters]] to inhibit the Desire Circuit. Without H&N activation, the Desire Circuit enters a runaway feedback loop of endless wanting.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** This is the source of automatic motivation. It is highly susceptible to "super-normal" stimuli (pornography, high-sugar foods).
- **Capability:** The Desire Circuit often overpowers the [[Control Circuit]], reducing the capability for executive restraint.
- **Leverage Points:** Introduce friction (Opportunity constraint) to delay action, allowing the [[Control Circuit]] to engage.
