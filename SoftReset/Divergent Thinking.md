# Divergent Thinking

**Definition:** A dopaminergic cognitive process associated with creativity that generates multiple, novel possibilities by suspending inhibition and increasing the salience of overlooked stimuli.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Part of the **Innovation Cycle**.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Dopamine]], [[Default Mode Network]], Cognitive Fatigue (which lowers inhibition).
- **Downstream (Drives):** Novelty, Raw material for [[Convergent Thinking]], [[Reward Prediction Error]].
- **Relationship:** Must be paired with [[Convergent Thinking]] (synthesis/logic) to produce actual productivity.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Enhanced by exposure to novelty (art, nature) and "mind wandering."
- **Leverage Points:** Use hypnagogic states (twilight sleep) to access the [[Default Mode Network]] when the [[Control Circuit]] is offline.
