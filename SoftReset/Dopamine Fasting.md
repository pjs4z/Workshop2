# Dopamine Fasting

**Definition:** An intentional intervention requiring abstinence from high-dopamine substances or behaviors for a set period (typically 30 days) to allow the brain to restore [[Homeostasis]].

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The fast is a "reset" button for the [[Pleasure-Pain Balance]].
1.  **Withdrawal Phase (Weeks 1-2):** The balance is tipped to pain. The subject experiences the [[Dopamine Deficit State]] (anxiety, irritability).
2.  **Restoration Phase (Weeks 3-4):** The "gremlins" dismount. Endogenous dopamine production normalizes. The capacity to enjoy simple pleasures returns.
3.  **Insight:** Abstinence provides clarity on the relationship between consumption and mental health that is impossible to see while in the cycle of use.

- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Dopamine Deficit State]], [[Anhedonia]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Homeostasis]], [[Insight]], [[Metacognition]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
