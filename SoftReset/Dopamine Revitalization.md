# Dopamine Revitalization

**Definition:** A behavioral protocol involving the voluntary restriction of high-stimulation activities to stimulate receptor upregulation and restore sensitivity to subtle rewards. It is not about eliminating dopamine, but restoring the balance between the [[Desire Circuit]] and [[Here and Now (H&N) Neurotransmitters]].

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Conscious decision via the [[Control Circuit]] or [[The Observing Self]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Reversal of [[Downregulation]], Increased receptor sensitivity, Enhanced appreciation of H&N stimuli.
- **Process:** Requires traversing a "withdrawal" period where the brain screams for the missing stimulus before homeostasis is restored.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Increases the long-term capability to enjoy life and resist impulses.
- **Leverage Points:** Combine with H&N activities (nature, connection) to buffer the discomfort of the fast.
