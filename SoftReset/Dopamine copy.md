# Dopamine

**Definition:** A neurotransmitter crucial for attention, motivation, and the experience of reward. In [[Attention Deficit Disorder]], receptor density and availability in the [[Orbitofrontal Cortex]] are low, often due to a lack of early [[Attunement]] which naturally stimulates its production.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Positive social interaction ([[Attunement]]), novelty, and interest.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Focus, motivation, and the feeling of being "alive."
- **Deficiency:** Low levels lead to "self-medicating" behaviors (addictions, risk-taking) to force a release.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Low dopamine makes "boring" tasks physically painful and cognitively impossible.
- **Leverage Point:** **Biological Priming**—Using exercise, novelty, or genuine interest to boost dopamine naturally before attempting difficult tasks.
