# Dopamine

**Definition:** A neurotransmitter that functions as the primary driver of motivation, reward prediction, and the pursuit of resources or possibilities existing in the extrapersonal space (the future or distance). It is not the chemical of pleasure, but of *wanting*.

Dopamine operates on the logic of "more," driving an organism to pursue resources that are not currently possessed. It functions through two distinct subsystems:
1. **[[Desire Dopamine Circuit]] (The Gas):** Located in the mesolimbic pathway, this generates raw motivation and unrefined urges based on potential value.
2. **[[Control Dopamine Circuit]] (The Steering):** Located in the mesocortical pathway, this utilizes dopamine to fuel [[Mental Time Travel]], calculation, and planning in the frontal lobes.

Dopamine is triggered by [[Reward Prediction Error]] (surprise) rather than certainty. It stands in direct opposition to [[Here and Now Chemicals]], often inhibiting the ability to enjoy the present moment while active.

- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Reward Prediction Error]], [[Evolutionary Mismatch]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Mental Time Travel]], [[Downregulation (Tolerance)]], [[The Hedonic Switchover]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** Dopamine provides the "Reflective Motivation" to plan for the future and the "Automatic Motivation" to chase novel stimuli.
- **Leverage Point:** To tame the "more" cycle, one must artificially dampen the signal of the [[Desire Dopamine Circuit]] by removing variable rewards (e.g., turning off notifications) to prevent the constant firing of anticipation.


## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Dopamine operates as the fuel for two distinct downstream systems: the [[Desire Circuit]] (impulse/craving) and the [[Control Circuit]] (planning/calculation). Its release is triggered by [[Reward Prediction Error]]—the surprise of an outcome exceeding expectations.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Reward Prediction Error]], Environmental Novelty, [[Reactance]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Desire Circuit]], [[Control Circuit]], [[Downregulation]] (if overstimulated), [[Divergent Thinking]].
- **Inhibitors:** [[Here and Now (H&N) Neurotransmitters]] (serotonin, oxytocin) which facilitate satiety and contentment.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** Acts as the automatic, reflexive drive for "more." It overrides homeostatic balance to pursue novel stimuli (e.g., social media scrolling, sugar, new romance).
- **Leverage Points:** To manage dopamine, one must engage in [[Dopamine Revitalization]] to reset receptor sensitivity or employ [[The Observing Self]] to decouple the feeling of wanting from the action of consuming.
