# Enforced Systemic Abstinence

**Definition:** A behavioral intervention requiring the prolonged, forceful deprivation of extreme dopaminergic stimuli. Its goal is to organically expand the temporal horizon of the reward system.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This acts as the "Architectural Restoration" Reinforcing Loop.

*   **Upstream (Driven by):** Conscious decision to reject [[Horizon Compression]].
*   **Downstream (Drives):** Increases the [[Vesting Period of Salience]].
*   **Outcome:** Restores the brain's ability to maintain an [[Inference Substrate]] across the silence between thought and action, reducing reliance on [[Forced Superposition]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
*   **Capability (Physical):** The only method to physically "repair" the container size of working memory for motivation.
*   **Protocol:** Requires removal of high-salience inputs (social media, video games, etc.) to reset the neurochemical baseline.
