# Evolutionary Mismatch

**Definition:** The maladaptive state arising when a biological system evolved for scarcity (the [[Dopamine]] system) operates within a modern environment of engineered abundance and super-stimuli.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The root cause of why [[Dopamine]] has transformed from a survival mechanism into a source of chronic dissatisfaction and addiction.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Technological progress, modern industrial abundance.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Chronic [[Dopaminergic Anticipation]], overload of [[Executive Modulation]] capacities.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Opportunity:** The physical and digital environment is designed to exploit this mismatch.
- **Leverage Point:** Environmental design (removing triggers) is necessary because biological willpower ([[Executive Modulation]]) is often insufficient against super-stimuli engineered to hijack this mismatch.
