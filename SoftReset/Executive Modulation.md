# Executive Modulation

**Definition:** The cognitive capacity to consciously regulate, suppress, or direct [[Dopaminergic Anticipation]] through reflective decision-making and self-discipline. It is the "brake" on the dopamine engine.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The primary mechanism for "Taming the Molecule of More."
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Circadian Regulation]] (biological fuel), [[Virtue Ethics]] (cognitive map).
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Eudaimonic Fulfillment]].
- **Inhibits:** Impulsive action derived from [[Dopaminergic Anticipation]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability (Psychological):** This is a finite resource that depletes with fatigue.
- **Leverage Point:** Must be supported by "Bio-Regulation Protocols" (Sleep/Diet) to function. Without biological support, [[Executive Modulation]] fails, leading to relapse into automatic dopaminergic behaviors.
