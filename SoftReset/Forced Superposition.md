# Forced Superposition

**Definition:** A state in which a distal macro-objective aligns perfectly with an immediate biological or psychological necessity (acute discomfort), creating an externalized [[Inference Substrate]] that compels action.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This mechanism serves as the "Balancing Loop" (The Survival Override) in a dysregulated system.

*   **Upstream (Driven by):** The crisis point reached after prolonged [[Tangential Unspooling]].
*   **Downstream (Drives):** Immediate, high-efficiency execution (hijacking the cognitive endowment).
*   **Mechanism:** The immediate survival need provides the "inference anchor" that the brain could not generate internally.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
*   **Opportunity (Physical/Social):** Relies on the environment to supply the motivation.
*   **Engineering Strategy:** Can be artificially manufactured ("Artificial Superposition") using commitment devices (financial penalties, social contracts) to compress consequences into the immediate present, bypassing the need for a healthy [[Vesting Period of Salience]].
