# Future-Oriented Anticipation

**Definition:** A cognitive and emotional state driven by the [[Internal Dopamine Factory]] that focuses resources on planning, abstraction, and the pursuit of future rewards (income, recognition, power). It is characterized by an "urge for more" rather than the enjoyment of the present.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This state is evolutionarily useful for survival but destructive in an environment of [[Societal Superabundance]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** External triggers (notifications, sugar) and an elevated [[Baseline Tolerance]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Catalyzes [[Homeostatic Downregulation]] and actively inhibits the [[Here and Now (H&N) System]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Differentiating this state from happiness is crucial. Dopamine promises pleasure but only delivers *arousal* and *anticipation*.
- **Leverage Point:** Recognizing that work and planning are inherently dopaminergic. True [[Dopamine Revitalization]] requires periods where "planning" is strictly forbidden to allow the H&N system to surface.
