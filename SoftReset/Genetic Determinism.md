# Genetic Determinism

**Definition:** The theoretical perspective that susceptibility to [[Addiction]] (specifically alcoholism) is primarily inherited through biological markers, a view contested by the Life Process model in favor of environmental and psychological factors.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Supports the **Disease Model Trap** by validating the concept of powerlessness.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Decreases [[Self-Efficacy]]; supports the [[Disease Model of Addiction]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Reduces psychological capability by suggesting biology is destiny.
- **Leverage Points:** Challenging this view with evidence of [[Natural Recovery]] and environmental influence.
