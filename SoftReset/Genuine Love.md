# Genuine Love

**Definition:** A union between individuals under the condition of preserving individual integrity. It is characterized by a commitment to mutual growth, fulfillment, and an active, non-fearful engagement with the world outside the relationship.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Genuine Love operates as an open system, exchanging energy with the environment rather than closing it off.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Maturing Out]] (emotional adulthood), high "Competence" (self-efficacy), and "Joy" (capacity for independent pleasure).
- **Inhibited by:** [[Interpersonal Addiction]] and the fear inherent in [[Learned Helplessness]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Enhanced engagement with the world; expansion of the self rather than contraction.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Requires "Mindfulness" to analyze motivations and "Competence" to stand alone.
- **Motivation:** Driven by "Integrity"—the desire for mutual independence over possession.
- **Leverage Points:** Promote "Social Architecture" (open households) to reduce the burden on single partners.
