# Here and Now (H&N) Neurotransmitters

**Definition:** A class of neurochemicals (including serotonin, oxytocin, and endorphins) that mediate sensory satisfaction, contentment, and enjoyment within the peripersonal space (the immediate present).

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
These are the antagonists to [[Dopamine]]. While dopamine drives "wanting," H&N chemicals drive "liking" and "having." They form the **Satisfaction Homeostasis Loop**.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Sensory experiences (touch, taste), Social bonding, Mindfulness.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Satiety, Contentment, Inhibition of the [[Desire Circuit]].
- **Interaction:** High H&N activity naturally suppresses the [[Desire Circuit]], breaking the cycle of constant craving.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** Provides the motivation to *stop* seeking and *start* savoring.
- **Leverage Points:** Deliberately engaging in H&N activities (e.g., gratitude practices, physical touch, "savoring" food) is a primary intervention for treating [[Downregulation]] and addiction.
