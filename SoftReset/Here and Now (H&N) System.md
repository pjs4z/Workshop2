# Here and Now (H&N) System

**Definition:** A neurochemical system involving neurotransmitters like oxytocin and endorphins that mediates feelings of fulfillment, social connection, and sensory satisfaction in the present moment. It acts as the functional opposite and counterbalance to the [[Internal Dopamine Factory]].

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The brain operates on a **Neurochemical Seesaw**:
1.  **Competitive Inhibition:** High levels of [[Future-Oriented Anticipation]] suppress the H&N system (you cannot savor a meal while obsessing over tomorrow's report).
2.  **Restoration:** Engaging the H&N system (e.g., through complex flavors, eye contact, touch) actively inhibits dopaminergic craving.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Dopamine Revitalization]] and [[Homeostatic Upregulation]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Feelings of satisfaction, satiety, and "enoughness."

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** This is the critical "missing half" of most fasting protocols. Deprivation without H&N stimulation leads to misery; deprivation *with* H&N stimulation leads to recovery.
- **Leverage Point:** Use **H&N Resources** (non-digital stimuli) to crowd out dopamine cravings. A smile, for example, can be as stimulating as chocolate but uses a different pathway that doesn't trigger the "urge for more."
