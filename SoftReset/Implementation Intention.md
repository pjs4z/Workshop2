# Implementation Intention

**Definition:** A specific plan made beforehand about when and where to act (e.g., "I will [BEHAVIOR] at [TIME] in [LOCATION]"), which utilizes specific cues to trigger a habit.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Mechanism:** Increases the clarity of the **Cue** in [[The Habit Loop]], reducing the cognitive load required to decide when to act.
- **Relationship:** A precursor to [[Habit Stacking]], where the "Time/Location" cue is replaced by a "Current Habit" cue.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Need for clarity over motivation.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Higher completion rates of [[Atomic Habit]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability (Psychological):** Removes the need for decision-making in the moment.
- **Opportunity:** explicitly defines the physical or temporal space for the behavior.
