# Inference Evaporation

**Definition:** The catastrophic cognitive phase transition where the macroscopic context ([[Inference Substrate]]) disappears from the attention frame at the exact moment focus shifts from meta-appraisal to tactical execution.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This phenomenon follows a "neurochemical corollary to thermodynamics," where the mental space allotted for a task is filled by immediate execution details, displacing the broader context.

*   **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Horizon Compression]] and an insufficient [[Vesting Period of Salience]].
*   **Downstream (Drives):** The "Vacuum of Decoupled Logic," leading directly to [[Tangential Unspooling]] and [[Involuntary Neurological Decoupling]].
*   **Mechanism:** The distal goal lacks the physiological weight to survive the shift in cognitive focus; it is crowded out by the high-bandwidth demands of the immediate sub-task.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
*   **Capability (Psychological):** Represents a structural failure in memory maintenance, not a refusal to act. The user loses the metric required to detect they are failing.
*   **Intervention:** Cannot be solved by willpower. Requires increasing the [[Vesting Period of Salience]] via [[Enforced Systemic Abstinence]].
