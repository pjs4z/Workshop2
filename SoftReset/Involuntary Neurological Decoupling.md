# Involuntary Neurological Decoupling

**Definition:** The structural separation of executive meta-appraisal (planning) from tactical execution (doing). The subject retains perfect rational understanding of the goal ("Reflective Alignment") but is physically unable to bridge the gap to action.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This is the overarching pathology described by the "Evaporation of the Inference Substrate."

*   **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Inference Evaporation]] and [[Horizon Compression]].
*   **Downstream (Drives):** The reliance on [[Forced Superposition]] for all major tasks.
*   **Distinction:** Unlike standard procrastination, this is not a failure of will or understanding; it is a failure of *connectivity* between the plan and the motor/cognitive action.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
*   **Motivation (Reflective vs. Automatic):** Highlights the total disconnect between Reflective motivation (wanting the goal) and Automatic motivation (the inability to initiate).
*   **Implication:** Moral exertion and "trying harder" are structurally impotent against this decoupling.
