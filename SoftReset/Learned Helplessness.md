# Learned Helplessness

**Definition:** A psychological disposition resulting from conditioning (often in childhood or institutional settings) where an individual believes their actions cannot influence outcomes. This passivity creates a susceptibility to [[Addiction]] as a substitute for mastery.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The root cause in the "Cycle of Atrophy."
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Overprotective parenting, institutionalization (schools/medicine), and lack of "Competence."
- **Downstream (Drives):** The need for [[Addiction]] (external support); susceptibility to [[Interpersonal Addiction]].
- **Mitigated by:** "Competence Engineering" (acquiring skills).

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Represents a severe deficit in psychological capability and self-efficacy.
