# Life Process Program

**Definition:** A therapeutic approach for overcoming [[Addiction]] that emphasizes [[Self-Efficacy]], values clarification, skill acquisition, and environmental modification rather than medical treatment or admission of powerlessness.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This program drives the **Life Process Learning Loop**, dealing with [[Relapse]] as data for skill improvement rather than defeat.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** A robust [[Value System]] and the desire for autonomy.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Increases [[Self-Efficacy]]; facilitates [[Natural Recovery]] and [[Controlled Usage]]; utilizes [[Community Reinforcement]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Focuses on building coping skills (communication, anger management).
- **Motivation:** Aligns behavioral change with deep personal values (Identity Construction).
- **Leverage Points:** Replaces the [[Disease Model of Addiction]] narrative to immediately boost agency.
