# Logotherapy

**Definition:** A therapeutic framework derived from Viktor Frankl positing that the primary human drive is the will to find meaning. It acts as a stabilizing force against the dissatisfaction inherent in the dopamine cycle.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Logotherapy provides a "meta-goal" that transcends the [[Desire Circuit]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Values, Philosophy, Religion.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Resilience, Long-term fulfillment (as opposed to fleeting happiness).
- **Mechanism:** By attaching meaning to suffering or effort, it allows the [[Control Circuit]] to maintain course even when the [[Desire Circuit]] is frustrated or bored.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** Provides the "Why" that enables a person to endure any "How."
- **Leverage Points:** Explicitly identifying virtues (wisdom, courage, etc.) aligns daily actions with higher purpose, buffering against the emptiness of the "dopamine letdown."
