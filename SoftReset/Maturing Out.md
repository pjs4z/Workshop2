# Maturing Out

**Definition:** The developmental phenomenon where addicts naturally cease their dependency as they acquire adult responsibilities, stable self-concepts, and the capacity to cope with life's challenges without artificial supports.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Represents the decay of the [[Addiction]] stock due to the growth of internal resources.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Acquisition of life skills, meaningful work, and "Competence."
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Genuine Love]] and [[Controlled Use]].
- **Opposite of:** The "Cycle of Atrophy" found in [[Learned Helplessness]].
