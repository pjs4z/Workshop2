# Minimum Viable Context

**Definition:** The absolute essential set of parameters, data points, and operational tools required to execute the *immediate first step* of a task. It explicitly excludes aesthetic adjustments, secondary research, or complex tool configurations.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
MVC acts as a "constraint" that lowers the activation energy required for [[Task Initiation]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** The need to bypass [[Meta-Optimization]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Rapid entry into [[Deep Work]].
- **Mechanism:** Reduces the "Choice Architecture" to a binary state: Work or Do Nothing.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Opportunity:** Physically/Digitally restructuring the environment. For example, opening a blank text file with only the header "Goal" rather than setting up a complex database.
- **Motivation:** Lowers the perceived difficulty of the task, preventing the "wall of awful" sensation often associated with large projects.
