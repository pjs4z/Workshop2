# Natural Recovery

**Definition:** The phenomenon wherein individuals overcome [[Addiction]] on their own through maturation, changing life circumstances, and personal resolve without formal medical treatment or participation in self-help groups.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Central to **The Virtuous Cycle of Natural Recovery**, where success in self-regulation builds confidence, leading to further success.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Value System]] (prioritizing health/family), [[Self-Efficacy]], and [[Community Reinforcement]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Long-term maintenance of Constructive Self-Regulation.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Target Behavior:** Constructive Self-Regulation.
- **Leverage Points:** Utilizing life transitions (marriage, parenthood) as natural restructuring events for daily routines.
