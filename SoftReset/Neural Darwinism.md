# Neural Darwinism

**Definition:** The competitive developmental process where brain circuits and neurons are strengthened by environmental usage and input, while unused pathways atrophy and die. This explains why [[Attention Deficit Disorder]] is environmental rather than strictly genetic—the brain wires itself according to the input it receives.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Sensory and emotional input from the [[Invisible Environment]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** The structural maturity of the [[Orbitofrontal Cortex]].
- **Implication:** If the brain uses [[Dissociation]] to survive, the circuits for attention and presence atrophy due to disuse.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Suggests that the ADD brain is not "broken" but "unwired."
- **Leverage Point:** Neuroplasticity implies that [[Self-Parenting]] and mindfulness in adulthood can re-initiate Neural Darwinism to build new regulatory circuits.
