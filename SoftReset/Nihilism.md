# Nihilism

**Definition:** The philosophical and psychological outlook that life lacks intrinsic meaning. It often emerges as a byproduct of the **Hedonic Treadmill**, where repeated cycles of [[Dopaminergic Anticipation]] followed by [[Hedonic Adaptation]] lead to the conclusion that effort is futile.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
The "Nihilistic Drift."
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Chronic [[Hedonic Adaptation]], lack of [[Virtue Ethics]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Depression, relapse into high-intensity [[Dopamine]] seeking (addiction) to numb the void.
- **Inhibited by:** [[Eudaimonic Fulfillment]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation (Negative Barrier):** A state of "learned helplessness" regarding satisfaction.
- **Leverage Point:** Must be actively countered by the "Choice of Meaning"—the intellectual decision to impose value on an indifferent universe through [[Virtue Ethics]].
