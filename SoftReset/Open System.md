# Open System

**Definition:** An environmental application or digital context, such as a social media feed, that provides a continuous, unbounded supply of external stimuli and high potential for distraction.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
An [[Open System]] is the environmental bedrock of modern distraction. It is engineered to exploit human biology, feeding directly into [[The Hedonic Treadmill of Stimulation]]. By presenting infinite novelty, it traps users in loops of checking behaviors. 
- **Upstream (Driven by):** The business models of tech companies engaging in [[Attention Hijacking]]; often accessed via a [[Scarcity Mindset]] (e.g., fear of missing out).
- **Downstream (Drives):** Catalyzes [[Attention Hijacking]] which subsequently triggers [[Dopamine]] release, spiking [[Stimulation Tolerance]] and ensuring sustained [[Overstimulation]]. 

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
In the COM-B model, an [[Open System]] provides the ultimate negative **Opportunity** for [[Compulsive Distraction-Seeking]]. Because these systems lack inherent [[Environmental Friction]], yielding to them requires almost zero effort. To engineer better behavior, an individual must systematically replace exposure to an [[Open System]] with a [[Closed System]] (e.g., an offline word processor).
