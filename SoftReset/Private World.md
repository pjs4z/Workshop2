# Private World

**Definition:** A closed social system constructed by addicts (or an addictive couple) characterized by a private standard of meaning and value. It validates their behavior while isolating them from external reality and feedback.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
A key component of the [[The Égoisme à Deux Spiral]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Interpersonal Addiction]] or group drug use.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Isolation; inability to cope with reality; eventual traumatic collapse when the bubble bursts.
- **Inhibits:** [[Genuine Love]] and reality testing.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Opportunity:** Functions as a closed loop that eliminates the opportunity for corrective feedback.
- **Leverage Point:** Must be disrupted by introducing third parties or separate activities.
