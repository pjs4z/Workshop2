# Self-Binding

**Definition:** The strategic creation of barriers between oneself and a drug of choice to mitigate [[Compulsive Overconsumption]]. It is an admission that willpower is a limited resource against [[Limbic Capitalism]].

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Self-binding introduces friction into the [[Pleasure-Pain Balance]] loop, interrupting the automaticity of the "wanting" phase. It engages the [[Prefrontal Cortex]] (planning) to override the Limbic System (impulse).

*   **Physical Binding:** Creating geographical or physical distance (e.g., locking a phone in a *kSafe*, unplugging the TV).
*   **Chronological Binding:** Restricting consumption to narrow time windows (e.g., "only checking email between 5 PM and 6 PM") to prevent [[Delay Discounting]].
*   **Categorical Binding:** Eliminating specific subtypes of triggers to avoid "cue-dependent learning" (e.g., avoiding sports radio to prevent gambling relapse).

- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Insight]], [[Dopamine Fasting]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Agency]], [[Delay Gratification]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
*   **Capability (Psychological):** Requires metacognition to predict future failure of will.
*   **Opportunity (Physical):** Modifies the environment to make the "bad" behavior harder to perform.
