# Self-Efficacy

**Definition:** The belief in one's own capacity to exert control over motivation, behavior, and the social environment, specifically regarding the ability to quit an [[Addiction]] and manage life problems.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
A critical stock in the **Virtuous Cycle of Natural Recovery**. High levels allow for [[Controlled Usage]] or [[Abstinence]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** The [[Life Process Program]], successful mastery experiences, and [[Community Reinforcement]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Inhibits [[Addiction]]; enables [[Natural Recovery]]. It is actively degraded by the [[Disease Model of Addiction]] and [[Genetic Determinism]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** A core psychological capability required to resist impulses.
- **Leverage Points:** Cognitive reframing from "addict" to "competent individual."
