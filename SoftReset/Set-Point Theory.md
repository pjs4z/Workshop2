# Set-Point Theory

**Definition:** The biological hypothesis that individuals have a genetically determined weight range that the body actively attempts to maintain, regulating metabolism and appetite to resist deviation.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Creates a **Balancing Loop (Biological Weight Homeostasis)** that resists weight loss efforts.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Genetics.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Can inhibit attempts at [[Controlled Usage]] regarding food if goals are unrealistic; triggers physiological hunger.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Understanding biological constraints helps in setting realistic goals.
- **Leverage Points:** Adjusting expectations to align with biological reality rather than impossible cultural ideals.
