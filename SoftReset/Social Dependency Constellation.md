# Social Dependency Constellation

**Definition:** A personality orientation, often found in the middle class, characterized by a need to cling to a single human object for support rather than developing diverse sources of gratification.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Nuclear family structures; cultural pressure to couple; lack of extended community.
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Interpersonal Addiction]]; the objectification of partners.
