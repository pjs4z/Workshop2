# Tangential Unspooling

**Definition:** A dysfunctional cognitive process where execution logic follows conceptually adjacent but globally irrelevant associations. This occurs because the cognitive space, emptied of its [[Inference Substrate]], backfills with complex but useless sub-tasks.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This is the active symptom of the "Vacuum of Decoupled Logic" loop.

*   **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Inference Evaporation]]. The brain creates an "illusion of optimality" to justify the drift.
*   **Downstream (Drives):** Time blindness and delay until [[Forced Superposition]] occurs.
*   **Mechanism:** Without the "North Star" to constrain the associative chain, the brain dissects tangents (e.g., researching font history instead of writing the essay) with absolute logical efficiency but zero strategic utility.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
*   **Motivation (Automatic):** The brain abhors a vacuum; it automatically fills the empty context window with high-complexity tasks that mimic productivity.
*   **Diagnosis:** Often misdiagnosed as "perfectionism" or "procrastination," but is actually a blind associative chain reaction.
