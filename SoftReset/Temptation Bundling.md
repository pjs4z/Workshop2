# Temptation Bundling

**Definition:** A strategy that links an action you *want* to do (high dopamine) with an action you *need* to do (low dopamine) to make the latter more attractive.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Mechanism:** Based on **Premack’s Principle**—more probable behaviors will reinforce less probable behaviors.
- **Integration:** Often combines with [[Habit Stacking]] (e.g., "After [HABIT I NEED], I will [HABIT I WANT]").
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Dopamine-Driven Feedback Loop]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** Increased attractiveness of [[Atomic Habit]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Motivation:** Hacks the brain's reward system to provide immediate gratification for a delayed-return behavior.
