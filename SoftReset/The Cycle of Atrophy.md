# The Cycle of Atrophy

**Definition:** A reinforcing feedback loop where [[Learned Helplessness]] leads an individual to seek a safe, predictable sensation ([[Addiction]]). As the individual relies more on the addiction, their engagement with the real world decreases, causing further atrophy of coping skills and increasing the initial helplessness.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Components:** [[Learned Helplessness]] -> [[Addiction]] -> Reduced Coping -> Increased [[Learned Helplessness]].
- **Leverage:** Intervene by building "Competence" (skills) to break the reliance on external props.
