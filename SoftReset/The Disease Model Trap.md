# The Disease Model Trap

**Definition:** A reinforcing feedback loop where belief in the [[Disease Model of Addiction]] leads to perceived powerlessness, reducing attempts at self-regulation and increasing the likelihood of failure, which then reinforces the original belief in the disease.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Components:** [[Disease Model of Addiction]] $\to$ Low [[Self-Efficacy]] $\to$ Failed Self-Regulation $\to$ Validation of Disease Model.
- **Intervention:** Break the loop by introducing the [[Life Process Program]] to restore agency.
