# The Four Steps (Plus One)

**Definition:** A cognitive-behavioral method adapted from OCD treatment to help addicts manually override [[Brain Lock]] by using conscious attention to rewire brain circuits (neuroplasticity).

## The Steps
1. **Re-label:** Identify the urge not as a "need" but as a "symptom" of addiction.
2. **Re-attribute:** Assign the urge to its source: "This is my brain sending me a false message due to old circuitry."
3. **Re-focus:** Buy time (15 mins) by engaging in a healthy, enjoyable alternative behavior.
4. **Re-value:** Clearly see the disaster the addiction brings and de-value the false gold of the impulse.
5. **Re-create (Plus One):** Choose a different life based on true values and intention.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Requires [[Compassionate Curiosity]] and mindful awareness.
- **Downstream (Drives):** Strengthening of the [[Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)]] and weakening of the automatic [[Addiction Process]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
- **Capability:** Provides a specific, trainable protocol to enhance psychological capability (self-regulation) in the moment of craving.
