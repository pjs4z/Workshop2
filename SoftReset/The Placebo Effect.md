# The Placebo Effect

**Definition:** A physiological or psychological response to an inert substance based on the subject's expectations and cultural set. It demonstrates that drug effects (and [[Addiction]]) are mediated by the user's mind and social context rather than chemistry alone.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Cultural beliefs, rituals, and "Set and Setting."
- **Downstream (Drives):** Can mimic [[Tolerance]] or relief from [[Withdrawal]].
- **Significance:** Proves that [[Addiction]] is an experience/interpretation, not just a biological imperative.
