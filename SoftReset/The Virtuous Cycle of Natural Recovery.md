# The Virtuous Cycle of Natural Recovery

**Definition:** A reinforcing feedback loop where a strong [[Value System]] motivates attempts at [[Controlled Usage]] or [[Abstinence]], leading to success that builds [[Self-Efficacy]], which in turn encourages further engagement with [[Community Reinforcement]], strengthening the original values.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Components:** [[Value System]] $\to$ Behavioral Attempt $\to$ Increased [[Self-Efficacy]] $\to$ [[Community Reinforcement]] $\to$ Stronger Values.
- **Outcome:** Sustainable [[Natural Recovery]].
