# The Égoisme à Deux Spiral

**Definition:** A reinforcing loop where a [[Social Dependency Constellation]] drives two individuals into an [[Interpersonal Addiction]]. To protect this dependency, they construct a [[Private World]] that excludes external feedback. This isolation makes them more dependent on one another for reality verification, thereby intensifying the addiction.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
- **Components:** [[Interpersonal Addiction]] -> [[Private World]] -> Isolation -> Increased Dependence -> [[Interpersonal Addiction]].
- **Leverage:** "Social Architecture" that forces interaction with the outside world.
