# Tolerance

**Definition:** The process by which an individual adapts to a stimulus so that increased exposure is required to maintain the same subjective effect or equilibrium. It shifts motivation from seeking positive pleasure to avoiding the pain of [[Withdrawal]].

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Part of the [[The Tolerance Trap]] balancing loop.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** The [[Opponent-Process]] (biological counter-reaction).
- **Downstream (Drives):** Escalation of [[Addiction]] intensity; loss of discrimination toward the object (quantity over quality).
