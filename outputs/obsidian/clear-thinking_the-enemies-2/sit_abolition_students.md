# Abolition Students
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frm_the_four_defaults|The Four Defaults Framework]]
**Summary:** Professor Robert George asks students if they would have opposed slavery in the antebellum South. While they claim yes, he argues they would have likely conformed to the norms of that time.
## Concept Manifestations
* [[conc_social_default|The Social Default]]: The overwhelming biological drive to conform to the majority view of one's time and place.
* [[conc_biological_instinct|Biological Instinct]]: The survival mechanism that prioritizes group belonging over moral independence.
