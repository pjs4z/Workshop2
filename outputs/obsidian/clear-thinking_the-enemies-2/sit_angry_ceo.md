# The Angry CEO
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frm_biological_reaction_model|The Biological Reaction Model]]
**Summary:** A CEO reacts with rage to an unscheduled call reporting a serious problem, refusing to listen. This ego-driven dismissal leads to the board firing him shortly after.
## Concept Manifestations
* [[conc_reaction_vs_reasoning|Reaction vs. Reasoning]]: The CEO's immediate wrath prevented him from processing the critical information logically.
* [[conc_ego_default|The Ego Default]]: His refusal to accept bad news or input that clashed with his beliefs because it threatened his self-image.
* [[conc_unforced_error|Unforced Error]]: Loss of his job was a self-inflicted wound caused by his inability to manage his reaction.
