# Benedict Arnold
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frm_the_four_defaults|The Four Defaults Framework]]
**Summary:** General Benedict Arnold betrayed the US after feeling underappreciated and bypassed for promotions, driven by a need to maintain a lavish lifestyle to signal status to his wife's wealthy family.
## Concept Manifestations
* [[conc_ego_default|The Ego Default]]: Deep resentment over lack of recognition and a desperate need to validate his self-worth through status.
* [[conc_hierarchy_instinct|Hierarchy Instinct]]: Jealousy toward younger officers promoted ahead of him and the need to climb the social ladder.
* [[conc_psychological_territory|Psychological Territory]]: Defending his identity as a member of the elite, leading to treason.
