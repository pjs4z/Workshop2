# Matthew Emmons
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frm_the_four_defaults|The Four Defaults Framework]]
**Summary:** Olympic shooter Matthew Emmons, poised to win gold, shot at the wrong target in the final round because he was focused on calming his nerves rather than checking the target number.
## Concept Manifestations
* [[conc_emotion_default|The Emotion Default]]: Focusing on managing internal anxiety (calming down) to the detriment of external awareness.
* [[conc_stimulus_response_gap|The Space]]: Skipping the crucial pause to verify the target number before pulling the trigger.
* [[conc_unforced_error|Unforced Error]]: Turning a guaranteed gold medal into an 8th place finish due to a lapse in focus.
