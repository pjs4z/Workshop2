# Mr. Rolex
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frm_the_four_defaults|The Four Defaults Framework]]
**Summary:** The author, working as a grocery clerk, was insulted by a rude customer with a fancy car. The author coped by mentally deciding he was 'better' than the customer because he wasn't a jerk.
## Concept Manifestations
* [[conc_hierarchy_instinct|Hierarchy Instinct]]: Both the customer (using wealth) and the author (using morality) organizing the world to feel superior to the other.
* [[conc_ego_default|The Ego Default]]: The author's need to rearrange his worldview to protect his self-esteem after an insult.
