# Parents Smoking
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frm_environmental_reprogramming|Environmental Reprogramming]]
**Summary:** The author's parents started smoking in the army to fit in and couldn't quit for decades until they changed their friend group.
## Concept Manifestations
* [[conc_willpower_fallacy|Willpower Fallacy]]: Their repeated failure to quit using discipline alone while staying in the same environment.
* [[conc_intentional_environment|Intentional Environment]]: Success came only when they moved to a new environment where the default behavior was not smoking.
* [[conc_social_default|The Social Default]]: Initially starting the habit solely to conform to their military peers.
