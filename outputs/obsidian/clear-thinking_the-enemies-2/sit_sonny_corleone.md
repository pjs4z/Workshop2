# Sonny Corleone
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frm_the_four_defaults|The Four Defaults Framework]]
**Summary:** In The Godfather, Sonny Corleone impulsively interrupts a meeting with a drug rival, undermining his father. Later, his hotheaded reaction to his brother-in-law leads him into a trap where he is assassinated.
## Concept Manifestations
* [[conc_emotion_default|The Emotion Default]]: Sonny's defining trait of reacting with rage and impulsivity rather than patience.
* [[conc_reaction_vs_reasoning|Reaction vs. Reasoning]]: Acting immediately on anger without calculating the strategic consequences.
* [[conc_unforced_error|Unforced Error]]: His death was the direct result of being baited into an emotional reaction.
