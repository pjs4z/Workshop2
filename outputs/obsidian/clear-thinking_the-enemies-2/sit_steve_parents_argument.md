# Steve's Argument
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frm_the_four_defaults|The Four Defaults Framework]]
**Summary:** A colleague named Steve had a heated political argument with his parents over dinner. They died in a car crash that night, leaving him with permanent regret over his last angry words.
## Concept Manifestations
* [[conc_emotion_default|The Emotion Default]]: Allowing political disagreement to trigger anger and harsh words that were not truly meant.
* [[conc_ordinary_moment|Ordinary Moment]]: A standard dinner conversation that turned into a defining life tragedy due to a lack of clear thinking.
