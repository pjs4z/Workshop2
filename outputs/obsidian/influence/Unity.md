# Unity

**Definition:** The experience of shared identity driven by tribe-like categories (kinship, locality, collaborative action) wherein individuals feel 'at one' with others, blurring the psychological boundaries between self and other.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
Unity goes beyond "liking" (similarities) to actual merged identities, driving extreme altruism, bias, and compliance.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** Significantly increased by shared suffering, geographical locality, and biological phenomena like [[Neuronal Cross-Excitation]].
- **Downstream (Drives):** Increases the power of [[Social Proof]] (we look to "our own" for cues). Central to [[The Unity-Biology Flywheel]].

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
Unity is the ultimate **Motivation** driver for Demonstrating In-group Favoritism and prosocial helping. 
- **Leverage Points:** Persuasive communications can artificially elevate Unity by reframing language to use partnership-raising pronouns ("we", "us") or by highlighting a mutual enemy, instantly triggering empathy-inducing [[Neuronal Cross-Excitation]].
