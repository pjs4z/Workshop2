# The Biomedical Evasion Cycle

**Definition:** A reinforcing feedback loop wherein psychological distress is systematically met with reductionist medical labels rather than relational repair, inadvertently perpetuating the original trauma.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This reinforcing loop traps individuals in a lifelong cycle of patienthood. The mechanism unfolds as follows:
1. **[[Psychic Suffering]]** drives the individual to seek medical relief.
2. The medical system applies a **[[Psychiatric Diagnosis]]**.
3. This diagnosis reinforces the paradigm of **[[Biological Determinism]]**, framing the pain strictly as a DNA-dictated chemical imbalance.
4. The heavy reliance on this biological narrative actively inhibits the exploration and repair of **[[Relational Connectedness]]** and obscures the root cause of **[[Childhood Adversity]]**.
5. Without the healing buffer of safe relationships, the underlying trauma remains unaddressed, which sustains and further increases the original **[[Psychic Suffering]]**, starting the cycle anew.

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
This systemic loop is maintained by the **Motivation** of both doctors and patients to avoid the painful emotional labor of confronting the "shadow side" of childhood trauma. It is structurally reinforced by the **Opportunity** drivers of a healthcare system that mandates the [[Psychiatric Diagnosis]] for treatment authorization.
- **Leverage Point:** Intervene at the point of diagnosis. By decoupling treatment access from abstract categorical diagnoses and instead mapping the patient's history of [[Childhood Adversity]], the loop can be broken and redirected toward building [[Relational Connectedness]].
