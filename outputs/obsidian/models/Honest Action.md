# Honest Action

**Definition:** The behavioral process of dismantling internal anxieties and defense mechanisms in order to act fearlessly and decisively on one's romantic and sexual desires.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Honest Action]] is the behavioral manifestation of courage. When an individual engages in [[Honest Action]], they initiate [[The Pain Period Homeostasis]], confronting their repressed shame. Surviving this period decreases [[Neediness]] and balances the psychological system. It is heavily reliant on the environmental context provided by [[Demographics]] and [[Honest Living]].
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Non-Neediness]], [[The Pain Period]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** [[Vulnerability]], [[The Pain Period]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
As a Target Behavior, [[Honest Action]] (Fearless Initiation and Physical Escalation) relies heavily on Capability and Opportunity drivers. 
- **Capability Modification:** Engineered through progressive desensitization. The subject is guided safely through [[The Pain Period]] using incremental social exposure (e.g., asking for the time, then transitioning to statements of intent). 
- **Opportunity Modification:** Achieved through Environmental Alignment. Redesigning the subject's sociocultural environment by mapping their [[Honest Living]] values to highly concentrated [[Demographics]] minimizes environmental [[Friction]] and increases the statistical probability of natural compatibility.
