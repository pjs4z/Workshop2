# Polarization

**Definition:** The behavioral process of expressing one's unapologetic truth to rapidly filter surrounding individuals into either highly receptive or highly unreceptive categories, thereby accelerating compatibility screening.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
[[Polarization]] functions as a Balancing feedback loop ([[The Polarization Filter]]) that actively regulates the dating ecosystem. By utilizing [[Honest Communication]] rooted in [[Non-Neediness]], an individual forces a quick assessment of compatibility. This drastically decreases [[Friction]] and filters out partners prone to [[Projection]], ensuring that [[Emotional Investment]] is only spent on highly receptive partners.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** [[Non-Neediness]], [[Honest Communication]]
- **Downstream (Drives):** Reduces [[Friction]], filters [[Projection]], accelerates [[Seduction]]

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
[[Polarization]] requires Motivation Modification (Rejection Reframing). To engineer this behavior, the subject must be trained to track rejections not as failures, but as successful system regulators that filter out [[Friction]]. By gamifying the screening process, the subject lowers their [[Neediness]]. Additionally, Capability is enhanced by replacing validation-seeking questions with assertive statements (Intent-Action Synchronization), reliably generating [[Sexual Tension]] while mitigating [[Creepiness]].
