# Pro-Change Verbalization (MET)

> [!abstract] Teleology & Agency
> **ufo:cultivates::** [[mom_willpower_limited|Willpower is Limited]] - *Verbalizing the need for change builds the empowering belief that willpower alone is insufficient and strategy is needed.*

> [!warning] Systemic Friction & Reality

## ⏱️ Execution Mereology
* **Pre-State:** Ambivalence regarding an addiction or bad habit.
* **Execution:** Speaking aloud the reasons for changing behavior ('pro-change statements') while avoiding arguments against change.
* **Post-State:** Strengthened internal motivation and belief in the necessity of the new behavior.
