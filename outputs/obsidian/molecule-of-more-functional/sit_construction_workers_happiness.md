# The Happiness of Construction Workers

> [!book] Narrative Summary
> A survey reveals construction workers are among the happiest employees. This is attributed to the balance of dopamine (planning/building/progress) and H&N (physical sensory engagement/camaraderie), creating a state of harmony often missing in purely abstract office jobs.
