# The Migration of the 7R Allele

> [!book] Narrative Summary
> Genetic studies reveal that the frequency of the 7R 'adventure' allele of the dopamine receptor gene increases with the distance a population has migrated from Africa. This suggests dopamine drove humans to explore new worlds and helped them survive in novel environments.
