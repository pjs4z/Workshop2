# The Parkinson's Patient Who Lost Everything

> [!book] Narrative Summary
> Ian, a retiree with Parkinson's, is prescribed a dopamine agonist (Cabaser). The artificial boost to his dopamine desire circuit causes him to compulsively gamble away his life savings, home, and car, despite having no prior history of gambling. The behavior ceases immediately upon stopping the medication.
