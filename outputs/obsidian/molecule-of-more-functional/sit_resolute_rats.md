# The Resolute vs. Lazy Rats

> [!book] Narrative Summary
> Rats with depleted dopamine are willing to eat food placed right in front of them (liking remains) but refuse to climb a small barrier to get it (wanting/motivation is gone). Normal rats work hard for the reward. This demonstrates dopamine provides the tenacity to overcome friction.
