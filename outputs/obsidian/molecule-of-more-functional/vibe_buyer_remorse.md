# Buyer's Remorse
**Experiencer:** The Consumer
**Trigger:** The moment of acquisition; moving an object from 'extrapersonal' to 'peripersonal' space.
**Appraisal:** The realization that the object is merely matter and does not deliver the promised future happiness.
**Somatic Substrate:** A drop in energy, a feeling of emptiness or nausea, and a cessation of the 'buzz'.
