# Cold Calculation
**Experiencer:** The Strategist / Dominator
**Trigger:** A high-stakes crisis or competition requiring victory.
**Appraisal:** The suppression of empathy and fear to maximize resource extraction and future success.
**Somatic Substrate:** Ice in the veins; a feeling of detachment, extreme focus, and lowered heart rate despite chaos.
