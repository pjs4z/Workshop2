# Companionate Warmth
**Experiencer:** The Partner / Parent
**Trigger:** Physical touch, familiar presence, safety in the here and now.
**Appraisal:** Deep satisfaction with the present moment; no desire for 'more'.
**Somatic Substrate:** Relaxation of muscle tension, release of oxytocin/endorphins, feeling of 'fullness'.
