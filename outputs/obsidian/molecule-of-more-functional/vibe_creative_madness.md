# Creative Madness / Flow
**Experiencer:** The Artist / Schizophrenic
**Trigger:** Low latent inhibition; the inability to filter out 'irrelevant' stimuli.
**Appraisal:** The perception of profound connections between unrelated things (salience).
**Somatic Substrate:** A flood of sensory data, racing thoughts, and a feeling of being overwhelmed or divinely inspired.
