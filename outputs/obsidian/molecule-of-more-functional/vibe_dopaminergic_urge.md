# The Itch of More
**Experiencer:** The Addict / Ambitious Executive
**Trigger:** A cue associated with a potential reward (e.g., a notification sound, a smell, a stock ticker).
**Appraisal:** The conviction that the next interaction will yield a reward greater than the current state.
**Somatic Substrate:** Restlessness, increased heart rate, visual tunneling, and a physical inability to sit still.
