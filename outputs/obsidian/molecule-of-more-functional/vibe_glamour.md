# Glamour
**Experiencer:** The Observer / Dreamer
**Trigger:** A distant, unattainable object (e.g., a mountain, a movie star, a future vacation).
**Appraisal:** The belief that the distant object is perfect and contains the solution to all dissatisfaction.
**Somatic Substrate:** A yearning ache, visual fixation, and suppression of immediate sensory flaws.
