# The Dopaminergic Lover
**Target Action:** Pursuit of the "Phantom" Partner

## The BDI Triad (Architecture of Action)
*   **Belief:** The beloved possesses the power to transform the future into a state of perfection.
*   **Desire:** Possession, novelty, and the thrill of the chase (Dopamine/Testosterone) rather than mere satisfaction.
*   **Intention:** To overcome all obstacles to acquire the target, often ignoring long-term compatibility or reality.

## Social Moments
*   Idealization of the partner.
*   Devaluation of the partner upon familiarity.
*   Infidelity as a strategy to renew [[entity_rpe|Reward Prediction Error]].
