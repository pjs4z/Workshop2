# 7R Allele
**Description:** The "Adventure Gene" (variant of DRD4). A biological marker reified as the driver of human migration, risk-taking, and the entrepreneurial spirit found in immigrant populations.

## Contextual Framing
*   **Migration Theory:** Societal progress is framed as a biological imperative driven by this allele.
*   **Agent Archetype:** The [[agent_migratory_pioneer|Immigrant-Entrepreneur]] who trades present stability for future possibility.
