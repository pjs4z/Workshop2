# The Control Circuit
**Description:** The mesocortical pathway (frontal lobes). The rational, calculating counterpart to desire. It utilizes tenacity, planning, and cold logic to dominate the environment and maximize future resources.

## Associated Qualia
*   **Cold Calculation:** The suppression of H&N empathy to execute utilitarian logic (e.g., The Trolley Problem).
*   **Vibe:** [[vibe_cold_calculated_detachment|Cold Calculated Detachment]].
