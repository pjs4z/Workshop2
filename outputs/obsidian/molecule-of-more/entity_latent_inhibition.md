# Latent Inhibition
**Description:** The brain's filter for irrelevance. Low latent inhibition is conceptualized as the physiological bridge connecting creativity, genius, and schizophrenia (madness).

## Qualia & Phenomenology
*   **Low Inhibition:** The overwhelming influx of sensory data; the ability to see connections where others see noise; the "dreamlike" state of waking life.
*   **Associated Agent:** [[agent_creative_genius|The Creative Genius]].
