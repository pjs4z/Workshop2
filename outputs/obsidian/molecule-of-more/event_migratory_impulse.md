# The Migratory Impulse
## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
*   **Pre-State:** Genetic predisposition ([[entity_7r_allele|7R Allele]]) or environmental dissatisfaction creates a "restless spirit"; devaluation of the "Here and Now".
*   **Execution:** Abandonment of familiar social support; high-risk movement into unknown environments; rapid adaptation to novel stressors.
*   **Post-State:** Establishment in new territory; potential for high achievement (entrepreneurship) or perpetual dissatisfaction and further movement.
