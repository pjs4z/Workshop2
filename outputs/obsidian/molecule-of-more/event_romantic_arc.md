# The Romantic Arc
## Mereology (Temporal Phases)
*   **Pre-State:** Dopaminergic arousal triggered by novelty and the "phantom" of the ideal partner; projection of a golden future (Extrapersonal space).
*   **Execution:** Intense pursuit and obsession (12-18 months); suppression of critical judgment; fusion of Testosterone (desire) and [[entity_dopamine|Dopamine]] (anticipation).
*   **Post-State:** Cessation of [[entity_rpe|Reward Prediction Error]] due to familiarity; Dopamine crash. Bifurcation point: Transition to [[entity_h_and_ns|Companionate Love]] or relationship dissolution.

**Associated Agent:** [[agent_dopaminergic_lover|The Dopaminergic Lover]]
