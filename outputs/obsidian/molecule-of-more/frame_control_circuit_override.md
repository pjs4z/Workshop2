# The Control Circuit Override
**Authorial Perspective:** Rationality is depicted not as a lack of passion, but as a cold, calculated form of dopamine that suppresses emotion to maximize future resources.

## Frame Elements
*   **Agent:** [[agent_control_strategist|The Rational Actor]] (The Spy, The Surgeon, The Trolley Problem Decider)
*   **Target:** Domination of the Environment / Maximization of Resources
*   **Trigger:** High-stakes dilemma or competitive threat
*   **Prior State:** Emotional arousal (Fear, Empathy, Panic)
*   **Outcome:** Calculated action, suppression of H&N impulses, and achievement of long-term goals

**Missing Variables:** Empathy, Warmth, Immediate sensory connection.
