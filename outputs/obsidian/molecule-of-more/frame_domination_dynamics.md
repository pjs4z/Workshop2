# Domination Dynamics
**Definition:** The use of the Control Circuit to manipulate the environment and others to secure resources.
## Defined Concepts
* [[conc_agentic_relationship|Agentic Relationship]] - A dopamine-driven social bond formed for the purpose of achieving a goal or utility, where others are viewed as extensions of the self.
* [[conc_affiliative_relationship|Affiliative Relationship]] - An H&N-driven social bond formed for the simple pleasure of companionship and social interaction.
* [[conc_cold_violence|Cold Violence]] - Calculated, emotionless aggression driven by the Dopamine Control Circuit to achieve a strategic end.
* [[conc_hot_violence|Hot Violence]] - Impulsive, emotional aggression driven by H&N (fear/anger) in response to immediate provocation.
