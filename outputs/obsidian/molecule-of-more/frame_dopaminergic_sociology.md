# Dopaminergic Sociology
**Definition:** The application of dopamine theory to explain migration, politics, and personality types.
## Defined Concepts
* [[conc_7r_allele|The 7R Allele (Adventure Gene)]] - A genetic variation of the dopamine receptor associated with risk-taking, novelty-seeking, and successful migration.
* [[conc_dopaminergic_personality|Dopaminergic Personality]] - A personality type characterized by high motivation, creativity, and future-focus, but often lacking in empathy and social connection.
* [[conc_balance|Balance (Harmony)]] - The optimal state of intermingling Dopamine (pursuit) with H&N (appreciation) to achieve true happiness.

**Satisfied By::** [[sit_7r_allele_migration|sit_7r_allele_migration]] - *The correlation between migration distance and the 7R allele supports the theory of dopaminergic genetics driving human exploration.*
