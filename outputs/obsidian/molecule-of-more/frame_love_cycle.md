# The Biological Cycle of Love
**Definition:** A framework mapping romantic relationships to the transition from Dopaminergic anticipation to H&N bonding.
## Defined Concepts
* [[conc_passionate_love|Passionate Love]] - The early, obsession-phase of a relationship driven by dopamine and the thrill of the unknown (lasts 12-18 months).
* [[conc_companionate_love|Companionate Love]] - The long-term phase of a relationship driven by H&Ns (Oxytocin/Vasopressin), characterized by deep satisfaction with the present reality.
* [[conc_glamour|Glamour]] - A dopaminergic illusion that makes the 'out of reach' seem perfect by obscuring the details of reality.

**Satisfied By::** [[sit_shawn_samantha_love_cycle|sit_shawn_samantha_love_cycle]] - *The narrative of Shawn and Samantha illustrates the transition from passionate dopamine-driven love to the fading of that thrill.*
