# The Migratory Imperative
**Authorial Perspective:** Migration is framed as a genetic destiny driven by the 7R allele, selecting for risk-takers who trade stability for possibility.

## Frame Elements
*   **Agent:** [[entity_7r_allele|The 7R Allele Carrier]] (Immigrant, Pioneer, Entrepreneur)
*   **Target:** The Unknown / New Territory / Opportunity
*   **Trigger:** Restlessness or environmental stressors
*   **Prior State:** Stasis in a familiar environment
*   **Outcome:** Survival through adaptation, resource maximization, or perpetual dissatisfaction

**Missing Variables:** Roots, Contentment with stasis, Risk aversion.
