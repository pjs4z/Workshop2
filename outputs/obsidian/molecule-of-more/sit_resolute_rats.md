# sit_resolute_rats
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frame_dual_dopamine_circuits|The Dual Dopamine Circuits]]
**Summary:** An experiment where dopamine-depleted rats refused to work for tasty treats (Bioserve tablets) but would still eat them if they were free, demonstrating that dopamine controls tenacity and effort, not enjoyment.
