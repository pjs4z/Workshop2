# sit_schultz_monkey_experiment
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frame_wanting_vs_liking|The Wanting vs. Liking Paradox]]
**Summary:** Wolfram Schultz's experiment with macaque monkeys where dopamine neurons eventually stopped firing upon receiving food and started firing upon seeing the light signal, proving dopamine is about anticipation rather than receipt.
