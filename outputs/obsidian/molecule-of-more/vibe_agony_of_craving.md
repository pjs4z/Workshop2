# The Agony of Craving
## Emotion Frame Elements
*   **Experiencer:** [[agent_addict|The Addict]]
*   **Trigger:** Exposure to a cue (person, place, thing) without the ability to consume.
*   **Appraisal:** Survival depends on this substance; its absence is a threat to existence.
*   **Physiological Substrate:** [[entity_dopamine|Dopamine]] deficit; "gnawing" need; physical distress.
*   **Social Display Rule:** Desperation, manipulation, willingness to sacrifice social bonds.
