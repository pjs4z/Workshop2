# Cold Calculated Detachment
## Emotion Frame Elements
*   **Experiencer:** [[agent_control_strategist|The High-Dopamine Strategist]] (e.g., Buzz Aldrin, Newton)
*   **Trigger:** A crisis requiring logical solution or a complex abstract problem.
*   **Appraisal:** Emotion is a liability; the situation must be dominated through logic and abstraction.
*   **Physiological Substrate:** [[entity_control_circuit|Control Circuit]] activation; suppression of norepinephrine (fear) and oxytocin (empathy).
*   **Social Display Rule:** Stoicism, lack of affect, "ice in the veins", social awkwardness.
