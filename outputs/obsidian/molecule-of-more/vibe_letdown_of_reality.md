# The Letdown of Reality
## Emotion Frame Elements
*   **Experiencer:** The Buyer / The Newlywed / The Winner
*   **Trigger:** The acquisition or consumption of the desired object (Possession).
*   **Appraisal:** The reality of the object is finite and less satisfying than the fantasy of its pursuit ([[entity_rpe|Reward Prediction Error]] = 0).
*   **Physiological Substrate:** Cessation of dopamine firing; drop in arousal.
*   **Social Display Rule:** Buyer's remorse, withdrawal, immediate scanning for a new target.
