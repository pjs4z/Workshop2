# The Thrill of Possibility
## Emotion Frame Elements
*   **Experiencer:** [[agent_dopaminergic_lover|The Lover]] / [[agent_addict|The Addict]] / [[agent_creative_genius|The Visionary]]
*   **Trigger:** A cue signaling potential reward (e.g., a text message, a new bakery, a stock tip).
*   **Appraisal:** This new object/person possesses the power to transform my future into perfection.
*   **Physiological Substrate:** [[entity_dopamine|Dopamine]] release in the mesolimbic pathway; increased energy and focus.
*   **Social Display Rule:** Manic energy, obsession, "leaning in", idealization of the target.
