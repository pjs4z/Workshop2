# Ancestor's Berry Bush
**Satisfies Frame:** [[frame_reward_prediction|The Reward Prediction Mechanism]]
**Summary:** An evolutionary ancestor walking the savanna stumbles upon a bush unexpectedly full of berries. The surprise triggers a rush of excitement and focuses her attention, locking the location into memory for future survival.
## Concept Manifestations
* [[conc_reward_prediction_error|Reward Prediction Error]]: The chemical blast of excitement caused specifically because the berries were a surprise (unexpected reward).
* [[conc_desire_circuit|The Desire Circuit]]: The survival mechanism that instantly sharpened her focus and encoded the memory of the bush.
