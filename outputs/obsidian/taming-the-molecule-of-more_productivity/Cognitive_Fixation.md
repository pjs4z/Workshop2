# [[Cognitive Fixation]]
**Type:** #BFO/DependentContinuant

## Concept Summary
[[Cognitive Fixation]] is a cognitive state of being mentally "stuck" or biased toward known, familiar solutions. It is characterized by a rigid, linear rationality where novel or "irrelevant" stimuli are automatically filtered out. This state is highly efficient for predictable tasks but is a primary obstacle to innovation.

This is the initial state that must be overcome (consumed) by the [[Creative Process]] in order to generate a novel output. It is a state borne by the [[Modern Human]].
