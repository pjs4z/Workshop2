# [[Familiarity]]
**Type:** #BFO/Dependent_Continuant

## Concept Summary
Familiarity is a Dependent Continuant; a state of being that cannot exist independently. It inheres within a Human Agent. It represents the fading of salience and reward that results from prolonged exposure to a stimulus. This state serves as the necessary fuel, or pre-condition, for the Dopaminergic Cycle to initiate.
