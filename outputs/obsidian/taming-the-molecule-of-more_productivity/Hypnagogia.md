# [[Hypnagogia]]
**Type:** #BFO/Dependent_Continuant

## Concept Summary
Hypnagogia is a specific neurological state borne by a Human Agent, occurring at the threshold of sleep. It is characterized by fluid, non-logical thought, making it a critical pre-condition for the Hypnagogic Capture process, which aims to extract creative insights generated during this state.
