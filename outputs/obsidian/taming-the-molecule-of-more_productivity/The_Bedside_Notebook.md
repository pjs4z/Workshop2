# [[The Bedside Notebook]]
**Type:** #BFO/Independent_Continuant

## Concept Summary
The Bedside Notebook is an Independent Continuant; a physical object. It is a tool that participates in the Hypnagogic Capture process. Its function is to serve as a medium for inscription, allowing ephemeral, unconscious insights to be reified into a stable, enduring form before they are lost.
