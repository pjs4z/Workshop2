# [[The 'Promise'/'Itch']]
**Type:** #BFO/Dependent_Continuant

## Concept Summary
The 'Promise' or 'Itch' is a desiderative state of anticipation and dissatisfaction that inheres within a Human Agent. It is not a process, but the *result* of a process (the Dopaminergic Cycle). It is the subjective experience of dopamine's forward-looking, reward-seeking function.
