# [[The Unconscious Mind]]
**Type:** #BFO/Independent_Continuant

## Concept Summary
The Unconscious Mind is a structurally distinct cognitive sub-system of the Human Agent. It is not directly accessible to conscious thought but acts as a parallel processor, particularly vital for the incubation of ideas and creative synthesis. It is a key participant in processes that require the dissolution of rigid, logical frameworks.
