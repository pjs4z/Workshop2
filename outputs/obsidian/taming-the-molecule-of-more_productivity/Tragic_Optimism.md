# [[Tragic Optimism]]
**Type:** #BFO/DependentContinuant

## Concept Summary
[[Tragic Optimism]] is the target mental state for the mature agent within this system. It is a complex disposition characterized by the ability to balance the biological drive for "more" with an appreciation for the "Here and Now." It represents the successful outcome of the process of [[Taming the Biological Imperative]].

This state is a conscious achievement, not a default condition. It requires acknowledging the indifference of the universe while still actively constructing meaning and purpose.
