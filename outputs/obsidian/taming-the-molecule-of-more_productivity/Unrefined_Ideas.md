# [[Unrefined Ideas]]
**Type:** #BFO/Dependent_Continuant

## Concept Summary
This Dependent Continuant represents the cognitive load of raw, unsorted, and low-salience information gathered during Divergent Thinking. It is a necessary intermediate state that must be borne by the Human Agent before it can be consumed and processed by Convergent Thinking.
