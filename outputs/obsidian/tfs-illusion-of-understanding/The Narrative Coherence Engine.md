# The Narrative Coherence Engine

**Definition:** A reinforcing feedback loop wherein cognitive limitations, such as restricting attention to available data, interact with retrospective biases to continuously generate flawed, highly coherent stories that culminate in a false sense of comprehensive worldly understanding.

## Systems Dynamics & Relationships
This system loop governs how human beings process complex historical realities. 
1. The heuristic `[[WYSIATI]]` artificially limits available information.
2. This limitation catalyzes `[[Sense-making]]` to bridge logical gaps with simple causal links, generating a `[[Narrative Fallacy]]`.
3. The `[[Halo Effect]]` reinforces this process by aligning all of an entity's traits to fit the story's outcome, exaggerating consistency.
4. This accumulation leads to a broad `[[Illusion of Understanding]]`, which gives false confidence that the limited information initially observed was indeed all there is to know, thereby deepening reliance on `[[WYSIATI]]`.

Concurrently, when assessing decisions, `[[Substitution]]` triggers `[[Hindsight Bias]]`, which increases `[[Outcome Bias]]`. This retrospective cycle directly feeds new `[[Halo Effect]]` designations, ensuring the Coherence Engine never runs out of fuel.
- **Upstream (Driven by):** `[[WYSIATI]]`, `[[Substitution]]`
- **Downstream (Drives):** `[[Illusion of Understanding]]`, `[[Outcome Bias]]`

## Behavioral Engineering Context (COM-B)
Because this loop is a self-reinforcing **Motivation** and **Capability** trap, it cannot be defeated purely by willpower. It requires structural **Opportunity** interventions: implementing "Blinded Evaluations" (shielding evaluators from recent outcomes when assessing leadership traits) and forcing statistical base-rate injection into corporate environments to forcefully sever the links between outcome, halo, and narrative.
